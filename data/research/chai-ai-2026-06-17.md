# SDR Research Brief — Chai AI (Chai Research Corp.)
**Prepared for Yuno | Date: 2026-06-17 | Analyst: Payments Intelligence**

> AI-companion / character-chat platform. chai.ml (web), CHAI iOS & Android apps, marketing/checkout site chai-ai.com. Founder-led, ~15–20 person team, recently crossed $80M ARR at a reported ~$2.4B valuation. Smaller / earlier-stage than Character.AI or Replika — this brief is candid where public data is thin.

---

## Executive Summary

Chai is a fast-growing AI-companion app (10M+ users, ~1M DAU, $80M ARR as of Q1 2026, ~$2.4B reported valuation) that monetizes almost entirely through subscriptions ([prnewswire.com](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/chai-ai-backed-by-coreweave-and-amd-hits-80m-arr-with-talks-of-2-4b-valuation-302759626.html), [pulse2.com](https://pulse2.com/chai-ai-surpasses-80-million-arr-at-2-4-billion-estimated-valuation/)). The bulk of revenue flows through Apple App Store and Google Play in-app purchases (Premium $13.99/mo, Ultra $29.99/mo, annual $134.99), but Chai also operates a **direct web checkout at chai-ai.com with distinct web-only pricing** ($159.99/yr, $9.99/wk, plus a token-metered "CHAI MAX" add-on), showing it is already trying to route subscribers off-store to escape the 30% platform tax ([chai-ai.com/pricing](https://www.chai-ai.com/pricing), [apps.apple.com](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chai-social-ai-platform-chat/id1544750895)). In April 2026 Chai began removing the free tier and enforcing **region-by-region paywalls**, monetizing aggressively across a global user base (US ~21–53%, plus Vietnam, Germany, Poland, UK, Russia, India) — exactly where local payment methods and cross-border acceptance drive conversion ([roborhythms.com](https://www.roborhythms.com/chai-ai-subscription-paywall-2026/), [similarweb.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/chai.ml/)). The public web-billing PSP is not disclosed and there is a heavy trail of double-charge / refund-difficulty complaints — both openings for a Yuno conversation on orchestrating web billing, adding APMs, and reducing involuntary churn.

## S10 — Strategic Insights

**1. Web billing already exists to dodge the 30% store tax — but it's single-rail.**
- **Evidence:** Web price ($159.99/yr) ≠ iOS price ($134.99/yr); direct "Chai AI Service Fee" descriptor; no orchestrator named ([chai-ai.com/pricing](https://www.chai-ai.com/pricing), [joinchargeback.com](https://www.joinchargeback.com/whats-this-charge/chai.ai/Chai-AI-Service-Fee)).
- **Pain:** Off-store revenue is the upside, but a single web PSP = no redundancy, weak local conversion, all eggs in one acquirer.
- **Yuno value:** One integration → multi-PSP routing + 1,000+ payment methods so the web funnel converts globally and never goes dark if one PSP fails.
- **Best case:** **Rappi** — scaled checkout across many markets/PSPs on one Yuno integration.
- **Angle:** "You're already steering subs to web to beat the 30% tax — let's make that web checkout convert like a local everywhere."

**2. Global users (VN/IN/PL/DE/RU) but US-domiciled, likely card-only web checkout.**
- **Evidence:** Traffic spread across Vietnam, India, Poland, Germany, Russia; single US Delaware entity; no local APMs verified ([similarweb.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/chai.ml/), [semrush.com](https://www.semrush.com/website/chai.ml/overview/)).
- **Pain:** Card-only checkout under-converts in UPI/BLIK/MoMo/Pix-first markets; cross-border declines and FX cost.
- **Yuno value:** Turn on local APMs market-by-market without per-market PSP contracts.
- **Best case:** **InDrive** — emerging-market local-payment coverage at scale via Yuno.
- **Angle:** "As you paywall by region, conversion will live or die on local methods — which ones are live on web today?"

**3. Heavy involuntary-charge / cancellation-desync complaints.**
- **Evidence:** Double annual charges, charges after cancellation, "active sub not found" ghosts, refund friction ([roborhythms.com](https://www.roborhythms.com/chai-ai-review/), [trustpilot.com](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/chatai.com)).
- **Pain:** Chargebacks, refunds, churn, support load, brand/Trustpilot damage.
- **Yuno value:** Unified subscription/billing state, sane retry & dunning, cleaner refund/chargeback tooling = fewer involuntary charges and disputes.
- **Best case:** **Livelo** — recurring-billing reliability and reconciliation at scale.
- **Angle:** "Your Trustpilot is full of double-charge and refund-loop stories — that's a reconciliation and routing problem we fix."

**4. Lean team (~11–20 engineers) carrying a $80M ARR billing surface.**
- **Evidence:** 15–20 engineers, $80M ARR ([pulse2.com](https://pulse2.com/chai-ai-surpasses-80-million-arr-at-2-4-billion-estimated-valuation/)).
- **Pain:** No bandwidth to build/maintain multi-PSP routing, APM integrations, dunning, and reconciliation in-house.
- **Yuno value:** One API offloads the entire payments roadmap so engineers stay on the AI product.
- **Best case:** **Reserva** — lean team scaling payments via a single Yuno integration.
- **Angle:** "At 15 engineers and $80M ARR, do you really want them building Stripe-plus-five-APMs plumbing?"

**5. Aggressive monetization shift (free-tier removal) raises conversion stakes.**
- **Evidence:** Free tier removed by region in 2026; ARR projected to $200M+ ([roborhythms.com](https://www.roborhythms.com/chai-ai-subscription-paywall-2026/), [prnewswire.com](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/chai-ai-backed-by-coreweave-and-amd-hits-80m-arr-with-talks-of-2-4b-valuation-302759626.html)).
- **Pain:** Every newly-paywalled region now depends on checkout converting — card-only and single-PSP leaves money on the table at the exact moment it matters most.
- **Yuno value:** Maximize authorization rates with retries/routing + local APMs as you scale paid globally toward $200M ARR.
- **Best case:** **Rappi** — auth-rate uplift via intelligent routing across PSPs.
- **Angle:** "You just made payment the gate to the whole product in new regions — let's make sure the gate opens."
