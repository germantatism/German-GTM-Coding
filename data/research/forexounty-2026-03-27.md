## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Limited Payment Method Coverage for Key Corridors

**Evidence:** Section 4 — Only bank transfers and Visa/MasterCard confirmed. No mobile money, no Pix, no local APMs for African/LATAM corridors.
**Pain Point:** Remittance recipients in Nigeria (OPay, mobile money), Ghana (MTN MoMo), Brazil (Pix) likely cannot receive funds via locally preferred methods.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API to enable 1,000+ payment methods including mobile money and Pix disbursements across all corridors.
**Best Case:** InDrive — 10 LATAM markets in <8 months, 90% approval rates.
**Outreach Angle:** "Forex County serves 7+ currency corridors but appears limited to cards and bank transfers — Yuno could unlock local payout methods like mobile money and Pix, reducing friction for recipients."

### Insight #2: No Identified PSP or Orchestrator — Single-Stack Risk

**Evidence:** Section 3 — No named PSP found; no orchestrator in use.
**Pain Point:** Likely running on a single acquirer, creating single-point-of-failure risk for cross-border card transactions.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing across multiple PSPs for redundancy and +7% approval uplift; 50% transaction recovery on failures.
**Best Case:** Livelo — +5% approval, 50% recovery.
**Outreach Angle:** "Cross-border remittance payments have high decline rates. Yuno's Smart Routing and transaction recovery could significantly improve your approval rates across corridors."

### Insight #3: Cross-Border Operations Without Local Entities

**Evidence:** Section 2 — UK entity only, serving 7+ destination markets.
**Pain Point:** All transactions are cross-border, which means higher decline rates, higher acquiring costs, and regulatory complexity.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Local acquiring in 200+ countries; new market live in weeks with no-code PSP enablement.
**Best Case:** Rappi — zero implementation time, 80% less analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle:** "Operating cross-border from the UK to 7+ markets likely means higher costs and lower approval rates than local acquiring. Yuno enables local processing without needing local entities."
