# McAfee — SDR Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-27 | **Prepared for:** Yuno Sales Team

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

McAfee is a global consumer cybersecurity company (~$1.92B annual revenue, ~17M monthly web visits) operating across 5+ regions with strong traffic in the US (26%), India (11%), and Brazil (4%). Their payment stack relies on **Digital River (DRI)** as merchant-of-record — a critical finding given Digital River GmbH's January 2025 insolvency filing. McAfee has severe, well-documented payment complaints (BBB "F" rating, 4,451 complaints in 3 years, active class action over auto-renewal billing), limited confirmed APMs (cards + PayPal globally), and no public evidence of a payment orchestration platform. Combined with a near-total C-suite refresh in 2024 (CEO/CPO both from Experian) and PE ownership incentivizing margin optimization, McAfee presents a strong Yuno opportunity around PSP migration risk, approval rate optimization on their massive recurring billing volume, and local APM expansion in high-traffic markets like India, Brazil, and Japan.

**Evidence:** S3 — Digital River GmbH filed insolvency Jan 2025; DRI is McAfee's confirmed MoR
**Pain Point:** European payment processing partner in financial distress; potential service disruption risk for a $1.9B subscription business

**Outreach Angle:** "With Digital River GmbH's insolvency, how is McAfee ensuring payment continuity in Europe? Yuno's orchestration layer provides instant PSP failover."

### Insight #2: Massive Recurring Billing = Approval Rate Optimization Goldmine
**Evidence:** S12 — ~$1.92B revenue, overwhelmingly subscription auto-renewals; S5 — thousands of failed charge complaints
**Pain Point:** High renewal-price delta (50–65% markup at renewal) means each failed renewal attempt = significant revenue loss. Failed charges drive complaints and churn.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart routing + 50% transaction recovery on failed payments. At McAfee's scale, even 1% improvement = ~$19M recovered revenue.
**Best Case:** Livelo achieved +5% approval uplift, 50% transaction recovery with Yuno
**Outreach Angle:** "At $1.9B in subscription revenue, what does a 1% improvement in renewal approval rates mean for McAfee's bottom line?"

### Insight #3: High-Traffic Markets Missing Local APMs
**Evidence:** S1 — India (11%), Brazil (4%) are top markets; S4 — only cards + PayPal confirmed globally; no Pix, UPI, Boleto verified
**Pain Point:** Cross-border card processing in India and Brazil = higher decline rates, higher costs, and missed conversion from customers preferring local methods
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API → Pix, Boleto (Brazil), UPI, NetBanking (India), Konbini (Japan) — no-code enablement
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates across LATAM with local acquiring via Yuno
**Outreach Angle:** "11% of McAfee's traffic comes from India where UPI dominates. How are you converting those visitors today?"

### Insight #4: C-Suite Refresh Creates Change Window
**Evidence:** S6 — CEO (Aug 2024), CPO (Dec 2024), CMO (Mar 2025) all new; both CEO and CPO from Experian (data/fintech background)
**Pain Point:** New leadership evaluating all vendor relationships; PE ownership (Advent/Permira) demands margin optimization

**Best Case:** Rappi achieved 80% less analyst resolution time with Yuno real-time monitoring
**Outreach Angle:** "New leadership often means fresh vendor evaluations. Yuno helps PE-backed companies like McAfee optimize payment costs across all markets from a single dashboard."

### Insight #5: Payment Complaints Creating Brand and Legal Risk
**Evidence:** S5 — BBB "F" rating, 4,451 complaints, class action, 3,000+ Pissed Consumer complaints
