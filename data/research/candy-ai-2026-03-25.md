## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Candy.ai is an AI companion/girlfriend chatbot platform operated by **EverAI Limited** (Malta), generating ~$25M ARR with 22.55M monthly visits — primarily from the US, France, and India. The company was acquired by **Futureverse** in April 2025 and its UK entity (CANDY AI LIMITED) was dissolved in March 2026. The payment stack is opaque — no PSP is publicly confirmed, though one third-party source reports Stripe integration signals. With significant billing complaints on Trustpilot (phantom charges, refund friction, unauthorized recurring billing), a restrictive 24-hour refund window, and multi-market traffic across 3+ continents, Yuno's smart routing and transaction recovery capabilities represent a strong fit — particularly to reduce chargebacks and optimize approval rates across diverse geographies.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Cross-Border Optimization in Top Markets
**Evidence:** S1 — US, France, India are top-3 traffic markets; S2 — no local entity in any of these countries; all traffic served from Malta-based EverAI.
**Pain Point:** Cross-border card transactions from Malta to US/France/India face higher decline rates and interchange fees.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing to local acquirers in top markets → +7% approval uplift; local acquiring reduces costs.
**Best Case:** Rappi-level real-time monitoring + InDrive-style multi-market enablement.
**Outreach Angle:** "With 22M+ monthly visits mostly from the US and France, routing all transactions cross-border from Malta is leaving approval rate points on the table."

### Insight #2: Chargeback & Transaction Recovery
**Evidence:** S5 — recurring Trustpilot complaints about phantom charges, unauthorized recurring billing, and refund friction; 24-hour refund window drives disputes to chargebacks.
**Pain Point:** High chargeback rates threaten merchant account stability — critical for high-risk category.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 50% transaction recovery; real-time fraud monitoring (Rappi: ms detection vs. 5–10 min manual).
**Best Case:** Livelo-style +5% approval with 50% recovery on failed transactions.
**Outreach Angle:** "The billing complaints pattern suggests recoverable revenue — companies like Livelo saw 50% transaction recovery with Yuno's retry logic."

### Insight #3: No Visible Orchestration Layer
**Evidence:** S3 — no public evidence of any orchestration platform; likely single-PSP dependency (probable Stripe).
**Pain Point:** Single PSP = single point of failure, no failover, no optimization across providers.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API → multiple PSPs, no-code enablement, failover routing.
**Best Case:** InDrive — 10 LATAM markets in <8 months, 90% approval, 4.5% recovery.
**Outreach Angle:** "Adding a second PSP with intelligent routing typically unlocks 3–7% approval uplift — without ripping out what's already working."

### Insight #4: High-Risk Category Needs PSP Diversification
**Evidence:** AI companion/NSFW category faces payment processor restrictions; Apple/Google ban native apps; competitors face same challenges.
**Pain Point:** High-risk merchants face sudden PSP terminations, higher fees, and limited processor options.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 300+ PSP connections including high-risk-friendly processors; rapid PSP switching if one drops service.
**Best Case:** Business continuity protection + cost optimization across multiple processors.
**Outreach Angle:** "In your category, having a backup PSP isn't optional — it's insurance. Yuno lets you add processors in days, not months."

### Insight #5: Untapped Local APMs in Key Markets
**Evidence:** S4 — India traffic confirmed but no UPI/Paytm; France traffic confirmed but no Carte Bancaire; Brazil has Pix but no Boleto.
**Pain Point:** Users in top markets may abandon checkout if preferred local payment method isn't available.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 1,000+ payment methods, local APM enablement in 200+ countries, no-code activation.
**Best Case:** New market live in weeks with full local APM coverage.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your Indian users likely expect UPI at checkout — enabling it could unlock significant conversion lift in your #3 market." *(Note: UPI availability not verified — frame as opportunity, not gap.)*
