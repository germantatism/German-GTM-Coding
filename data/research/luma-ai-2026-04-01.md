## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Luma AI is a $4B-valued AI video generation company (lumalabs.ai) with 30M+ users globally, backed by $1.07B in funding including a $900M Series C led by Saudi Arabia's HUMAIN. They run a **single-PSP stack on Stripe** with no payment orchestration layer, processing subscription revenue (~$21M annually) across credit cards, debit cards, bank payments, and Cash App Pay. The company is rapidly expanding internationally — new offices in London and Riyadh, 200+ hires planned — creating immediate payment complexity (multi-currency, local APMs in MENA/EMEA) that Yuno is purpose-built to solve.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Single-PSP Dependency at Scale
**Evidence:** Section 3 — Stripe is the sole PSP with no orchestration layer, no failover.
**Pain Point:** With 30M+ users and growing international operations (US, UK, Saudi Arabia), a single PSP creates concentration risk. Any Stripe outage = 100% payment downtime.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing across multiple PSPs provides redundancy and +7% approval uplift. No-code PSP enablement means adding backup acquirers in weeks.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates and 4.5% transaction recovery with multi-PSP routing.
**Outreach Angle:** "As Luma scales to $4B+ with 30M users on a single PSP, a Stripe outage means zero revenue collection. Companies like InDrive solved this by adding routing across PSPs — without rebuilding their payments stack."

### Insight #2: MENA Expansion Without Local Payment Methods
**Evidence:** Section 6 — Riyadh office opened Feb 2026; HUMAIN partnership; Publicis MENA deal. Section 4B — No MENA APMs verified (mada, STC Pay, SADAD).
**Pain Point:** Luma is investing heavily in MENA (office, partnerships, Arabic foundation model) but checkout appears to be global Stripe with no local Saudi payment methods.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno enables mada, STC Pay, SADAD, and other MENA APMs via single integration. New market live in weeks.
**Best Case:** Rappi went live in new markets with zero implementation time.
**Outreach Angle:** "With the Riyadh office and HUMAIN partnership, Luma is doubling down on MENA — but local payment methods like mada are table stakes for Saudi consumers. Yuno can enable these in weeks."

### Insight #3: India & Brazil Traffic Without Local APMs
**Evidence:** Section 1 — India is #1 traffic market, Brazil is #3. Section 2 — No local entities in either. Section 4B — UPI (India) and PIX (Brazil) not verified.
**Pain Point:** Two of the three largest markets by traffic likely have suboptimal payment conversion due to cross-border processing and absence of dominant local APMs (UPI dominates India at 80%+ digital payments; PIX dominates Brazil).
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno connects to local acquirers in India and Brazil, enabling UPI, PIX, Boleto, and 200+ other local methods. Cross-border → local acquiring = higher approval rates.
**Best Case:** Livelo saw +5% approval uplift and 50% transaction recovery in Brazil.
**Outreach Angle:** "India and Brazil are Luma's #1 and #3 markets by traffic — but without local acquiring, every transaction is cross-border with lower approval rates. Companies like Livelo gained +5% approval by switching to local processing."

### Insight #4: Billing Complaints Signal Subscription Management Gaps
**Evidence:** Section 5 — Trustpilot 1.9/5, charges after cancellation, refund friction, unresponsive support.
**Pain Point:** Ghost charges and cancellation issues damage brand trust and increase chargeback risk. Manual support can't scale with 30M+ users.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's real-time payment monitoring (similar to Rappi deployment — ms detection vs. 5-10 min manually) can flag anomalous subscription charges immediately, reducing disputes.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved 80% less analyst resolution time with real-time monitoring.
**Outreach Angle:** "Luma's Trustpilot shows recurring billing issues — charges after cancellation, slow refunds. Real-time payment monitoring can catch these before they become chargebacks."

### Insight #5: iOS Revenue Leakage via Apple Tax
**Evidence:** Section 3A — iOS payments via Apple IAP (15-30% commission). Section 8 — No documented web-to-mobile optimization.
**Pain Point:** Every iOS subscriber pays Apple's 15-30% commission. At scale, this is significant revenue leakage.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart payment routing can optimize web vs. mobile payment flows, and Yuno's checkout experience can be designed to encourage web subscriptions over iOS IAP. **[INFERENCE — this is a broader business strategy, not purely a payment orchestration play]**
**Outreach Angle:** Best used as a secondary talking point in combination with the core multi-PSP and local APM arguments.
