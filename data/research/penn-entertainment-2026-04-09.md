# SDR Research Brief — Penn Entertainment (PENN)
**Date:** 2026-04-09 | **Framework:** v8.0 | **Analyst:** Yuno Payments Intelligence

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Penn Entertainment, Inc. (NASDAQ: PENN) is a $6.96B/year North American gaming and entertainment company operating 43+ casino properties across 20 US states, online sports betting in 21 jurisdictions via theScore Bet, and iCasino in 5 states via Hollywood Casino. Headquartered in Wyomissing, PA with ~23,400 employees and a $1.9B market cap, Penn is the largest regional casino operator in the US with a 33-million-member PENN Play loyalty program. Their digital transformation is accelerating: iCasino revenue grew 40%+ YoY in 2025, and the Interactive segment reached $1.3B in annual revenue. After ending the $1.5B ESPN Bet deal early, Penn relaunched under its own theScore Bet brand (Dec 2025). Their digital properties (sports betting deposits/withdrawals, iCasino transactions, loyalty cashless payments) process significant payment volume across multiple states — a strong use case for payment orchestration to optimize approval rates, manage multi-state regulatory compliance, and consolidate PSP relationships.

## SECTION 6 — Digital Payments & Technology Infrastructure

### Payment-Relevant Operations
- **Sports Betting Deposits/Withdrawals:** theScore Bet processes deposits and withdrawals across 21+ US states, each with different regulatory requirements for payment methods, KYC, and geolocation
- **iCasino Transactions:** Hollywood Casino Online processes real-money gaming transactions in PA, MI, NJ, WV, and Ontario
- **Cashless/Cardless Technology:** Penn has deployed "3 C's" technology (Cardless, Cashless, Contactless) across physical casino properties, enabling digital wallet payments at slot machines and table games
- **PENN Cash:** Digital currency within PENN Play loyalty program, usable for gaming, dining, entertainment, and Ticketmaster ticket purchases
- **In-House Game Studio:** PENN Game Studios develops proprietary casino games, indicating significant tech investment

### Payment Complexity Indicators
- Multi-state operations require compliance with different state gaming commissions
- Online sports betting and iCasino require real-time deposit/withdrawal processing
- Multiple payment methods needed: credit/debit cards, ACH/eCheck, PayPal, Venmo, Apple Pay, Play+ prepaid cards
- High-volume recurring micro-transactions from sports betting (deposits for individual bets)
- Cross-state loyalty program (PENN Play with 33M members) requires unified payment infrastructure
- Canadian operations (Ontario) add cross-border payment complexity
