## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Ryanair is Europe's largest ultra-low-cost carrier (€13.95B revenue, 200M passengers in FY2025), operating in 40+ countries primarily across Europe and with a growing footprint in Morocco. Their payment stack is mature by European airline standards: they adopted APEXX Global as a payment orchestration layer in June 2
022, reporting a +11% authorization rate improvement and -10% processing cost reduction at the time. However, three years on, Ryanair's checkout still shows structural gaps — they only added BLIK in Poland as recently as October 2025, Pay by Bank coverage only launched in December 2024, and their Morocco/MENA expansion (5 bases, $1.4B invested) has no confirmed local APM coverage. The Yuno opportunity is a competitive displacement + MENA/expansion play: replace an aging APEXX integration with superior routing, recovery rates, and Africa-ready APM coverage.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

---
**Insight #1: APEXX Is 3.5 Years Old — Benchmarks Have Drifted**
**Evidence:** APEXX partnership June 2022 (Section 3); reported +11% auth rate and -10% cost at launch.
**Pain Point:** Orchestration benchmarks from 2022 are not 2026 benchmarks. The industry has moved — Yuno clients are seeing +7% auth rate improvements and 50% transaction recovery *on top of* existing orchestration. After 3 years, APEXX's routing rules become stale, and there's no competitive pressure to optimize if Ryanair is locked into a single orchestrator.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's real-time routing engine, anomaly detection, and recovery flows deliver measurable improvement *versus an existing orchestrator baseline*, not just versus no orchestration. Rappi reduced analyst resolution time by 80% and detected anomalies in milliseconds vs. 5–10 minutes with their previous setup.
**Best Success Case:** Rappi — similarly high-volume, multi-market, already using payment infrastructure but upgraded to Yuno for operational efficiency and real-time monitoring.
**Outreach Angle:** "Ryanair adopted APEXX in 2022 and drove an 11% authorization improvement — impressive. What's the current baseline, and when was the routing logic last benchmarked? We're seeing airlines and travel platforms extract another 5–7% on top of their existing orchestrator when they add Yuno's routing layer."

**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno enables local acquiring in new markets in weeks, no-code PSP enablement. CMI connectivity + Wafacash + local card scheme support out of the box. Same model used by InDrive to go live across 10 LATAM markets in <8 months.
**Best Success Case:** InDrive — expanded into new emerging markets at speed with Yuno handling local PSP complexity.
**Outreach Angle:** "Ryanair just made a $1.4B bet on Morocco. Are local Moroccan card acceptance rates at the level they need to be — or are you still processing cross-border from an Irish acquiring entity?"
