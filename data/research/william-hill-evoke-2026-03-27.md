# SDR Research Brief — William Hill (Evoke plc)
**Date:** 2026-03-27
**Analyst:** Claude (Yuno Payments Intelligence)
**Framework:** v8.0

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

William Hill is one of the most recognized sports betting brands globally, now operating under Evoke plc (formerly 888 Holdings). Evoke reported FY2025 revenue of ~GBP 1.79B (+2% YoY) but faces severe headwinds: a 22% revenue decline in certain segments, UK Remote Gaming Duty rising from 21% to 40%, planned closure of up to 200 UK retail shops (affecting ~1,500 jobs), and an active strategic review that may result in a sale or breakup of the company. Despite these pressures, Evoke operates across 20+ regulated markets with brands including William Hill, 888casino, 888poker, Mr Green, and SI Sportsbook. No evidence was found of a third-party payment orchestration platform — given 95% of revenue now comes from regulated markets (following the sale of the US-facing 888 business to Hard Rock Digital and acquisition of Romania's Winner.ro), the multi-jurisdictional payment complexity is significant. The company is a strong prospect for payment orchestration but timing should account for the ongoing strategic review/potential sale.

### Payment Orchestration in Gambling — What We Found

**No confirmed usage of Spreedly, Primer, Gr4vy, CellPoint, APEXX, or Yuno was found among any of the gambling/betting companies listed above.** This is a significant finding — the sports betting vertical appears to be an underserved market for payment orchestration vendors.

Key observations:
- **Spreedly** actively markets to the gambling vertical with dedicated content on optimizing gambling payment gateways ([Spreedly blog](https://www.spreedly.com/blog/how-to-optimize-gambling-payment-gateways))
- **Gr4vy** positions itself for gambling payments complexity ([Gr4vy](https://gr4vy.com/posts/top-payment-challenges-for-2026-and-how-payment-orchestration-solves-them/))
- **CellPoint Digital and APEXX** are named in payment orchestration market reports but no confirmed gambling operator clients were found ([BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20231221694782/en/Payment-Orchestration-Platform-Market-Size-Share-Trends-Report-2023-2030))
- **PayAtlas** lists major payment providers for sports betting (Adyen, Stripe, Worldpay, Nuvei, Paysafe) but no orchestration layer ([PayAtlas](https://payatlas.com/industry/sports-betting-4948))
- Most major operators appear to manage multi-PSP complexity in-house

## SECTION 4 — PAYMENT COMPLEXITY SIGNALS

### Why Evoke/William Hill Needs Payment Orchestration

1. **Multi-market complexity:** Operating in 20+ regulated markets, each with unique payment requirements, local APMs, and compliance rules
2. **95% regulated revenue:** High regulatory burden means payment compliance across every jurisdiction
3. **Multiple brands:** William Hill, 888casino, 888poker, 888sport, Mr Green, SI Sportsbook, Wink Bingo — each potentially with separate payment stacks
4. **Cost pressure:** With GBP 73M in cost savings over 2024-2025, payment optimization via orchestration could deliver significant savings
5. **Strategic review:** A potential buyer/investor would value a modernized, unified payment stack
6. **AI and automation focus:** The company has explicitly referenced implementing AI and automation to streamline operations — payment orchestration fits this narrative

### Risks / Timing Considerations

- **Strategic review underway:** A sale or breakup could mean the decision-maker changes
- **Cost-cutting mode:** May resist new vendor spend unless ROI is immediately clear
- **Job cuts:** Internal champions may be displaced
- **S&P Negative outlook:** Financial stress could delay technology investments
