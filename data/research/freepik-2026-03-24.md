# Freepik — SDR Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-24 | **Analyst:** Yuno Payments Intelligence

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Freepik (freepik.com) is a Malaga, Spain-based creative content platform with 80–100M+ monthly users and ~800K paying subscribers, backed by EQT, Eurazeo, and Atwater Capital. The company operates Freepik, Flaticon, Slidesgo, Storyset, and EyeEm across a global user base with top markets in India, the US, and Brazil. Their confirmed payment stack includes credit/debit cards, PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay — with strong signals pointing to **Stripe** as the underlying PSP (confirmed for sister product Flaticon). **No payment orchestration layer was found.** Yuno's opportunity lies in smart routing for cross-border transactions across 200+ countries, local APM enablement for high-traffic markets (India, Brazil, LATAM), and helping Freepik optimize approval rates and reduce the payment complaints visible on Trustpilot.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Cross-Border Revenue Leakage in Top Markets
**Evidence:** India (#1 traffic) and Brazil (#3 traffic) have no local entity; all transactions processed cross-border from Spain.
**Pain Point:** Cross-border card transactions face 5–15% higher decline rates and elevated interchange fees.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing to local acquirers in India and Brazil → +7% approval uplift; local acquiring = lower costs.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval across 10 LATAM markets in <8 months with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your top traffic market (India) and #3 (Brazil) are likely running cross-border — that's leaving approval rate and revenue on the table."

### Insight #2: No Payment Orchestration = Single-PSP Dependency
**Evidence:** Strong Stripe signal (confirmed for Flaticon); no orchestration layer detected.
**Pain Point:** Single-PSP dependency means no failover, no smart routing, limited APM flexibility. If Stripe goes down, all payments go down.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API → 300+ PSPs; failover routing; no-code PSP enablement.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved zero implementation time and 80% less analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle:** "With 800K+ paying subscribers across 200+ countries, a single PSP creates a single point of failure — and likely leaves approval uplift untapped."

### Insight #3: Missing Local APMs in High-Growth Markets
**Evidence:** Only cards, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay confirmed. No UPI (India), Pix (Brazil), iDEAL (Netherlands), or other local methods verified.
**Pain Point:** In India, UPI accounts for 70%+ of digital transactions. In Brazil, Pix surpassed cards. Without local APMs, Freepik loses potential conversions.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 1,000+ payment methods across 200+ countries, enabled via single API with no-code configuration.
**Best Case:** Livelo achieved +5% approval and 50% transaction recovery with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "In your #1 market, UPI is how most people pay — and in Brazil, Pix already surpassed cards. These are quick wins for conversion."

### Insight #4: Payment Complaints Signal Subscription Lifecycle Issues
**Evidence:** Trustpilot shows recurring complaints about double charges, unauthorized upgrades, and refund friction.
**Pain Point:** Payment-related complaints erode trust and increase churn; suggests gaps in subscription management and dispute handling.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Real-time transaction monitoring (Rappi: ms detection vs. 5–10 min manually); automated retry and recovery.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved 80% less analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle:** "I noticed some Trustpilot reviews mentioning billing issues — real-time monitoring and smarter retry logic can catch these before they become chargebacks."

### Insight #5: Enterprise Tier + China Expansion = New Payment Complexity
**Evidence:** Freepik launched Enterprise tier (May 2025) with credit-based B2B billing; CEO identified China as largest untapped market.
**Pain Point:** Enterprise billing requires invoicing, PO management, multi-seat licensing. China requires Alipay, WeChat Pay, UnionPay.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Support for B2B payment workflows + 1,000+ methods including Chinese APMs, enabled in weeks.
**Best Case:** New market live in weeks with no-code PSP enablement.
**Outreach Angle:** "As you scale Enterprise and eye China, the payment complexity grows fast — Yuno can help you go live with local methods in weeks, not months."
