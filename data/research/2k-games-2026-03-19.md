## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

2K Games is a major video game publisher (subsidiary of Take-Two Interactive, TTWO) behind franchises like NBA 2K, Civilization, Borderlands, and WWE 2K. Take-Two's FY2025 revenue was $5.63B, with ~79% ($4.45B) from recurrent consumer spending (virtual currency, microtransactions). 2K operates a direct-to-consumer store (store.2k.com) powered by **Xsolla as Merchant of Record** with **Stripe** for card processing — a dual-PSP setup with no payment orchestration layer detected. The main Yuno opportunity lies in optimizing 2K's growing direct-to-consumer channels (store.2k.com, nba2kmobile.com web store), where they bypass platform fees but lack smart routing, failover, and regional APM optimization across 190+ countries.

**Analysis:** The #1 complaint is VC (Virtual Currency) not being delivered after successful payment — players pay but the currency never appears in-game. 2K Support acknowledges it can take 24+ hours to resolve. This points to fragmented payment reconciliation between the payment processor, game servers, and platform APIs. A unified payment orchestration layer with real-time webhooks and transaction monitoring (like Yuno's real-time monitors, similar to what Rappi uses) could dramatically reduce resolution time.

3DS failures causing payment declines are also common — a smart routing engine could retry with alternative PSPs or route to acquirers with better 3DS pass-through rates.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

**Insight #1: No Payment Orchestration on Growing D2C Channels**
**Evidence**: Section 3 — Stripe + Xsolla dual-PSP setup, no orchestrator detected.
**Pain Point**: 2K runs store.2k.com and nba2kmobile.com with a rigid dual-PSP setup. No smart routing, no failover, no dynamic PSP selection based on geography or card type.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Single API to orchestrate across Stripe, Xsolla, and additional PSPs. Smart routing could uplift approval rates by up to 7%, critical as D2C revenue grows.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — 10 markets in <8 months, 90% approval rate, 4.5% recovery rate
**Outreach Angle**: "Your direct store runs on Stripe + Xsolla with no routing layer — as D2C grows to avoid platform fees, smart routing could immediately uplift approval rates."

**Insight #2: VC Delivery Failures = Fragmented Payment Reconciliation**
**Evidence**: Section 5 — #1 customer complaint is paying for Virtual Currency but not receiving it. 2K Support says resolution takes 24+ hours.
**Pain Point**: Payment confirmation from PSP/platform doesn't synchronize with game servers in real time. This creates chargebacks, support tickets, and destroyed trust.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Real-time transaction monitoring and webhooks (like Rappi implementation — anomaly detection in milliseconds vs. 5-10 min manually) could flag and resolve delivery failures instantly.
**Best Success Case**: Rappi — 80% reduction in analyst resolution time, real-time anomaly detection
**Outreach Angle**: "VC delivery failures are your #1 customer complaint — real-time payment monitoring could cut resolution from 24 hours to seconds."

**Insight #3: Play OS Real-Money Contests Need Compliant Payment Infra**
**Evidence**: Section 6 & 7 — NBA 2K "Play OS" introduces real-money entry fees and prize payouts. Active lawsuits around microtransaction spending.
**Pain Point**: Real-money gaming contests require robust payment compliance, responsible gaming guardrails, and potentially different payment rails (payouts, not just collections). Current Xsolla MoR model may not be built for this.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Yuno supports both collections and payouts, with fraud tools and compliance monitoring across 190+ countries. Purpose-built for complex payment flows.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — multi-market payout infrastructure across LATAM
**Outreach Angle**: "Play OS real-money contests are a new payment vertical — the payout and compliance requirements are fundamentally different from game sales."

**Insight #4: Mobile Web Store Strategy to Bypass App Store Fees**
**Evidence**: Section 3 — nba2kmobile.com web store routes through checkout.2k.com, bypassing Apple/Google's 30% commission.
**Pain Point**: As more revenue shifts to mobile web purchases (avoiding platform fees), 2K needs a world-class checkout experience optimized per region — or they lose the very savings they're trying to capture to poor conversion rates.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Unified Checkout Builder per region, local APM enablement, smart routing — maximize conversion on the channel where margins are best.
**Best Success Case**: Livelo — +5% approval rate, 50% transaction recovery
**Outreach Angle**: "Your NBA 2K mobile web store saves on platform fees, but is the checkout optimized for each market? Local APMs and smart routing could meaningfully lift conversion."

**Insight #5: $4.45B in Recurring Payment Volume with No Orchestration**
**Evidence**: Section 12 — 79% of Take-Two's $5.63B revenue comes from recurrent consumer spending. In-game purchases are the business.
**Pain Point**: Even a 1% improvement in payment approval rates on $4.45B in recurring volume = $44.5M in recovered revenue.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Smart routing delivers up to 7% approval uplift. Transaction recovery captures an additional 50% of initially failed transactions.
**Best Success Case**: Livelo — +5% approval rate on recurring transactions
**Outreach Angle**: "At $4.5B in recurring payment volume, even a 1% approval uplift is worth $44M — smart routing and transaction recovery make that realistic."
