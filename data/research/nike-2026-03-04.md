## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Nike (NYSE: NKE) is the world's largest athletic footwear and apparel company — $46.3B revenue in FY2025 across 190+ countries — currently in the middle of a painful digital turnaround after 8 consecutive quarters of declining online sales. Adyen is their confirmed primary acquirer globally, with Klarna and Afterpay layered for BNPL. However, no payment orchestration layer has been identified, meaning Nike has no unified routing, failover, or transaction recovery logic across their multi-market DTC stack. With a new CEO (Elliott Hill, "Win Now" strategy), a new COO overseeing tech, and DTC digital revenue down 20% in FY2025, every basis point of checkout conversion is mission-critical — and the absence of orchestration is a directly addressable gap Yuno can close.
