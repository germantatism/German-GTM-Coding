## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

DoorDash (NASDAQ: DASH) is the dominant US food delivery platform (67% market share) with $13.7B in FY2025 revenue and operations across 40+ countries via three brands: DoorDash (US/CA/AU), Wolt (Europe/Asia), and Deliveroo (UK/Europe/Middle East — acquired May 2025 for $3.9B). Their primary PSP is **Stripe Connect** across both DoorDash and Wolt, with **no public evidence of a third-party payment orchestrator**. The 2026 integration of three distinct tech stacks across 40+ countries — flagged by management as a "high investment intensity" year — represents a significant payment orchestration opportunity, particularly for local APM optimization, smart routing, and cross-border payment complexity.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Three Tech Stacks, Zero Orchestrator — 2026 Integration Window

**Evidence:** DoorDash is merging three distinct payment stacks (DoorDash/Stripe Connect + Wolt/Stripe + Deliveroo/unknown) across 40+ countries in 2026, flagged as a "high investment intensity" year. No third-party orchestrator found.
**Pain Point:** Managing multi-PSP routing, local APM support, and regulatory compliance across three brands without a unifying orchestration layer creates massive engineering overhead and approval rate variability.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API to unify all three stacks, smart routing across PSPs, no-code enablement of local APMs per market — dramatically reducing integration time and cost during the merge.
**Best Case:** InDrive scaled 10 LATAM markets in <8 months with 90% approval rates via Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "DoorDash is merging three payment stacks across 40+ countries this year — Yuno helped InDrive do exactly that across 10 LATAM markets in under 8 months with 90% approval rates."

---

### Insight #2: Double-Charge & Chargeback Pain at Scale

**Evidence:** Widespread double-charge complaints across Reddit, Trustpilot, JustAnswer. Chargebacks911 published a dedicated DoorDash merchant chargeback guide — systemic issue on both consumer and merchant sides.
**Pain Point:** Payment processing errors at DoorDash's scale (~$75B+ GOV) mean even a small error rate translates to millions in chargebacks, refund costs, and customer churn.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart routing for higher approval rates (+7% uplift), 50% transaction recovery on failed payments, real-time monitoring (Rappi case: ms-level detection vs 5-10 min manually).
**Best Case:** Livelo achieved +5% approval uplift and 50% transaction recovery with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your Trustpilot is flooded with double-charge complaints — at $75B+ GOV, even a 1% improvement in payment reliability saves tens of millions. Livelo saw 50% transaction recovery with Yuno."

---

### Insight #3: European APM Fragmentation Across 30+ Markets

**Evidence:** Wolt already supports MobilePay (Finland), Kaspi (Kazakhstan), meal vouchers (Finland/Slovakia/Romania), cash (Hungary/Slovakia/Kazakhstan). Deliveroo adds UK, France, Italy, Belgium, Ireland, UAE — each with distinct local APM requirements (Carte Bancaire, PostePay, Open Banking, Mada).
**Pain Point:** Each new market requires individual PSP integrations, local payment method enablement, and regulatory compliance — multiplicative engineering effort across 30+ countries.
**Yuno Value Prop:** No-code PSP enablement, new market live in weeks, 1,000+ payment methods via single API.
**Best Case:** InDrive launched 10 LATAM markets in <8 months with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "Wolt and Deliveroo serve 30+ European markets, each needing local APMs — Yuno connects 1,000+ payment methods via one API, letting you go live in new markets in weeks, not months."

---

### Insight #4: DashPass Subscription Billing Failures

**Evidence:** High volume of complaints about unauthorized DashPass charges, failed cancellations, and free-trial-to-paid conversion issues. Non-account-holders being charged (potential card fraud or dark-pattern enrollment).
**Pain Point:** Involuntary churn from subscription billing failures, customer trust erosion, chargeback exposure on recurring payments.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Orchestration-level retry logic and smart routing for recurring payments can reduce involuntary churn and improve subscription payment success rates.
**Outreach Angle:** "DashPass billing failures are driving chargebacks and churn — orchestrated retry logic across multiple PSPs can recover failed subscription payments before they become cancellations."

---

### Insight #5: No LATAM Presence — Massive Untapped Market

**Evidence:** DoorDash has zero presence in Latin America. Competitors like Rappi and iFood dominate. LATAM food delivery market is growing rapidly with high mobile penetration.
**Pain Point:** If/when DoorDash expands to LATAM, they'll face an entirely new payment ecosystem (Pix, Boleto, OXXO, PSE, Nequi, etc.) with no existing infrastructure.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno was built for LATAM — deep expertise in local payment methods, PSP connections, and regulatory compliance across the region. InDrive and Rappi are both Yuno clients.
**Outreach Angle:** "When DoorDash looks at LATAM, you'll need Pix, Boleto, OXXO, and dozens of local methods — Yuno already powers payments for Rappi and InDrive across the region."
