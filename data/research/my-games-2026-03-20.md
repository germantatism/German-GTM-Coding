## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

MY.GAMES is a Dutch-registered global video game publisher and developer, spun off from Russian tech conglomerate VK (formerly Mail.ru Group) for $642M in September 2022. The company operates 50+ live games across mobile, PC, and console — flagship titles include War Robots ($1B+ lifetime revenue as of Feb 2025), Rush Royale (~$470M+ lifetime revenue), and Hustle Castle (80M+ downloads) — serving 1.2+ billion registered users across 128+ countries via ~10 internal studios and ~1,500 employees. CEO Elena Grigorian was appointed in 2023 following the restructuring. The confirmed payment stack includes Xsolla (web market, likely MoR), Adyen (European APMs — Sofort/Klarna/Trustly), BoaCompra and PagSeguro (Brazil), PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Paysafecard, and platform store billing — but no payment orchestrator has been publicly identified. CRITICAL SIGNAL: In February 2026, Elena Emelyanova (Head of Payments, MY.GAMES) published an interview in The Paypers explicitly naming high cross-border card decline rates, fragmented local APM ecosystems, currency volatility in Brazil and Turkey, and the need for "dynamic routing to orchestrate multiple payment options" as live operational challenges. MGVC — the company's $100M VC arm — also explicitly identified "D2C payment solutions" as a key 2025 investment focus. Yuno's primary opportunity: payment orchestration across 50+ live games, cross-border complexity in 128 countries, confirmed multi-PSP fragmentation without a routing layer, Brazil/Turkey currency pain confirmed by Head of Payments, China re-entry requiring new APM rails, and documented purchase/refund complaints pointing to retry and recovery gaps.
