## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

HelloFresh SE (FRA: HFG) is the world's largest meal kit company, operating across **18 countries** with €6.8B in FY 2025 revenue and an estimated 100M+ annual transactions. Their confirmed payment stack includes **Adyen** (primary acquirer, global), **PayPal Braintree** (PSP-agnostic vault & orchestration), **Checkout.com** (new global partner, Oct 2025), and **NAB** (Australia). The main Yuno opportunity lies in **smart routing across their multi-PSP stack** (Adyen + Checkout.com + Braintree), **decline recovery optimization** for their subscription model (where involuntary churn directly impacts LTV), and **expanding local APM coverage** as they launch Factor in European markets. Their €300M efficiency program and new CFO (Fabien Simon, Nov 2025) signal openness to payment cost optimization.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Multi-PSP Complexity Without Dedicated Orchestration
**Evidence:** Section 3 — Adyen (primary acquirer) + Checkout.com (new global partner, Oct 2025) + PayPal Braintree (vault/orchestration) + NAB (Australia). Four PSPs confirmed across 18 markets.
**Pain Point:** Managing routing decisions, token synchronization, and failover logic across 4 PSPs without a dedicated orchestration platform creates operational overhead and suboptimal routing.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API to orchestrate Adyen, Checkout.com, Braintree, and NAB — smart routing to maximize approval rates while minimizing processing costs.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates across 10 LATAM markets with Yuno orchestration.
**Outreach Angle:** "You're already running a multi-PSP strategy with Adyen, Checkout.com, and Braintree. An orchestration layer could optimize routing decisions across all three — our merchants see +7% approval uplift when we add smart routing."

### Insight #2: Subscription Churn from Failed Payments
**Evidence:** Section 5 — aggressive 24-hour auto-retry on declined payments; PayPal case study cites +3% approval rate as "directly correlated to delivering more meal kits"; Emina Zahirovic (Assoc. Director Global Payments) states "our aim is to continuously optimize how we process payments."
**Pain Point:** Every failed recurring payment is a potential churned customer. Current retry logic is PSP-specific rather than cross-PSP failover.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 50% transaction recovery via smart retry across PSPs. If Adyen declines, retry via Checkout.com or Braintree automatically.
**Best Case:** Livelo recovered 50% of failed transactions; Rappi reduced analyst resolution time by 80%.
**Outreach Angle:** "For a subscription business processing 100M+ transactions, even a 1% improvement in authorization rates translates to thousands of recovered customers. We've helped subscription merchants recover 50% of initially failed transactions through cross-PSP smart retry."

### Insight #3: European RTE Expansion Creates New Payment Flows
**Evidence:** Section 6 — Factor launching European production hub Q1 2026; already live in Sweden/Denmark (2024), Germany (2025). Different brand, different checkout, different markets.
**Pain Point:** New brand (Factor) in new European markets requires local APM integration, local acquiring, and SCA compliance — all while maintaining the HelloFresh payment stack.
**Yuno Value Prop:** No-code PSP enablement for new markets; live in weeks instead of months. Pre-built integrations for European APMs (iDEAL, Bancontact, MobilePay, Vipps).
**Best Case:** InDrive launched 10 LATAM markets in under 8 months with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "As Factor expands across Europe, each new market brings local payment methods and acquiring requirements. A single orchestration layer could help you go live faster — our merchants launch new markets in weeks, not months."

### Insight #4: €300M Efficiency Program + New CFO = Cost Optimization Window
**Evidence:** Section 6 — €300M annual efficiency program (H2 2024), new CFO Fabien Simon (Nov 2025), revenue declining -9% in FY 2025, meal kit AEBITDA margin at 13.5% (highest since pandemic).
**Pain Point:** Payment processing costs are a significant line item for a €6.8B business. Multi-PSP routing without optimization means overpaying on interchange and processing fees.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart routing to lowest-cost acquirer per transaction while maintaining approval rates. Real-time cost monitoring across PSPs.
**Best Case:** Reserva achieved +4% approval in under 3 months while optimizing costs.
**Outreach Angle:** "With Fabien taking over as CFO and the efficiency program in full swing, payment cost optimization across your PSP stack could be a quick win. Our merchants typically see meaningful processing cost reduction through smart routing to the lowest-cost acquirer per transaction."

### Insight #5: Regulatory Pressure on Subscription Billing Practices
**Evidence:** Section 5 — $7.5M California settlement, ACCC lawsuit in Australia (62,061 customers billed post-cancellation), forced payment details before viewing plans.
**Pain Point:** Multiple jurisdictions now scrutinizing HelloFresh's billing practices. Transparent payment management and audit trails become critical.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Centralized payment dashboard with real-time monitoring (Rappi case: ms detection vs. 5–10 min manually) provides audit trail and anomaly detection across all PSPs.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved zero implementation time and 80% less analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle:** "With regulatory attention on subscription billing across US and AU, having a unified view of all payment transactions across PSPs helps ensure compliance and rapid issue detection."
