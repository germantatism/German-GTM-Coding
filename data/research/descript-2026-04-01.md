# Descript — SDR Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-04-01 | **Analyst:** Yuno Payments Intelligence

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Descript is an AI-powered video and audio editing platform headquartered in San Francisco, with ~186 employees across 5 continents and an estimated ~$100M+ ARR growing 30%+ YoY. The company uses **Stripe as its sole PSP** with no payment orchestration layer, billing exclusively in USD. With ~36% of traffic from the US but significant international reach (UK, India in top 3), Descript forces all non-US customers through cross-border card payments with bank FX conversion — a clear friction point. The single-PSP dependency, USD-only billing, limited payment methods (credit card + Link by Stripe only), and growing enterprise motion create multiple Yuno opportunity angles around local acquiring, multi-currency billing, and payment optimization.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Single-PSP Dependency on Stripe
**Evidence:** S3 — Stripe confirmed as sole PSP across all billing, payments, and retry logic.
**Pain Point:** Single point of failure for all payment processing. If Stripe experiences downtime or rate changes, Descript has no fallback. No ability to route transactions to optimize approval rates.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing across multiple PSPs → +7% approval uplift. Failover routing eliminates single-PSP risk.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates across 10 LATAM markets with Yuno's multi-PSP routing.
**Outreach Angle:** "With ~$100M+ ARR running through a single PSP, even a 1% improvement in approval rates could mean $1M+ in recovered revenue. Companies like InDrive used Yuno's smart routing to hit 90% approvals."

### Insight #2: USD-Only Billing with Global User Base
**Evidence:** S1 — 63%+ traffic from outside the US; S3 — bills in USD only with bank FX conversion.
**Pain Point:** International customers (UK #2, India #3) face bank FX fees and potential declines from cross-border card processing. Higher decline rates on cross-border transactions vs. local acquiring.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Local acquiring in 200+ countries. Process in local currency to reduce FX friction and improve approval rates.
**Best Case:** Reserva saw +4% approval improvement in <3 months with local acquiring via Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "With over 60% of your traffic outside the US but USD-only billing, your international customers face FX friction. Yuno enables local acquiring so you can charge in local currency and boost approval rates."

### Insight #3: Limited Payment Methods for International Markets
**Evidence:** S4 — Only credit cards and Link by Stripe confirmed. No PayPal, UPI, BNPL, or local APMs.
**Pain Point:** India users (#3 traffic) can't pay with UPI (dominant method). UK users can't use Open Banking or PayPal. Missing local APMs = lost conversions.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API → 1,000+ payment methods. Enable local APMs per market with no-code configuration.
**Best Case:** New market live in weeks with no-code PSP enablement.
**Outreach Angle:** "As Descript grows internationally, enabling local payment methods like UPI in India or Open Banking in the UK could unlock conversion from users who don't use international credit cards."

### Insight #4: Billing Complaints & Failed Payment Recovery
**Evidence:** S5 — Multiple Trustpilot complaints about post-cancellation charges, refund friction, and failed payment handling.
**Pain Point:** Stripe's retry logic alone isn't sufficient. Billing issues create support burden and drive churn. AI credits model adds complexity.
**Yuno Value Prop:** 50% transaction recovery rate. Real-time monitoring (Rappi: ms detection vs 5–10 min manual) reduces support tickets and involuntary churn.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved 80% less analyst resolution time with Yuno's real-time monitoring. Livelo recovered 50% of failed transactions.
**Outreach Angle:** "Billing complaints on Trustpilot suggest failed payment recovery is a pain point. Yuno's real-time monitoring helped Rappi cut analyst resolution time by 80% — and our recovery engine captures 50% of otherwise-lost transactions."

### Insight #5: Enterprise Growth Creates Payment Complexity
**Evidence:** S6 — CEO transition, Head of Partnerships hire, Enterprise tier expansion with SSO. S2C — Mid-market customer spend ~$3,000/mo (2x+ growth YoY).
**Pain Point:** Moving upmarket means invoice billing, multi-currency needs, compliance requirements, and more complex payment flows. Current Enterprise-only ACH/invoice is likely manual.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Automated invoice billing, multi-currency support, and enterprise-grade payment infrastructure through single integration.
**Best Case:** Yuno's no-code PSP enablement lets companies add payment methods and currencies in weeks, not months.
**Outreach Angle:** "As Descript pushes upmarket with Enterprise, payment complexity will grow — multi-currency invoicing, local acquiring, compliance. Yuno handles all of that through a single API."
