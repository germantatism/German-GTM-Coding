# SDR Research Brief — Tipsport
**Date:** 2026-03-27 | **Prepared for:** Yuno Sales Team | **Framework:** v8.0

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Tipsport a.s. is the dominant sports betting and online casino operator in the Czech Republic (~$877M group revenue, 95% online), with a secondary presence in Slovakia via Tipsport SK. The company processes massive digital payment volume across cards, instant bank transfers (10+ Czech banks, 5+ Slovak banks), e-wallets (PayPal, Skrill, Aircash), and its proprietary Pluso wallet. **No payment orchestrator was found in public evidence.** Tatra banka is the confirmed card acquirer for Slovakia; no Czech acquirer was identified. Key Yuno opportunity: multi-market payment complexity (CZ/SK with different banks, currencies CZK/EUR, and regulatory regimes), deposit-only card processing (withdrawal asymmetry), and no visible failover routing — all pain points orchestration solves.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Deposit/Withdrawal Asymmetry Creates Orchestration Need
**Evidence:** S4 — Cards are deposit-only in both CZ and SK; withdrawals route through bank transfer, Skrill, or Pluso.
**Pain Point:** Managing separate deposit and withdrawal flows across 15+ bank integrations and multiple e-wallets increases operational complexity and reconciliation burden.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Unified orchestration layer normalizes deposit/withdrawal routing, reducing integration maintenance from 15+ point-to-point connections to one API.
**Best Case:** InDrive — 10 LATAM markets, 90% approval, 4.5% recovery.
**Outreach Angle:** "Managing 15+ bank integrations across two markets with separate deposit/withdrawal flows is exactly the complexity Yuno was built to simplify."

### Insight #2: No Visible Card Acquirer Redundancy
**Evidence:** S3A — Only Tatra banka confirmed as SK card acquirer; CZ acquirer unknown; no multi-PSP routing evidence.
**Pain Point:** Single-acquirer dependency means any downtime or decline spike has no failover. For a company where 95% of revenue is online, even minutes of card processing downtime = significant revenue loss.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing across multiple acquirers → +7% approval uplift + automatic failover.
**Best Case:** Livelo — +5% approval, 50% recovery.
**Outreach Angle:** "With 95% of revenue flowing through online payments, even a 1% approval uplift on card deposits would be material to your bottom line."

### Insight #3: Multi-Currency Complexity (CZK + EUR)
**Evidence:** S2 — CZ entity operates in CZK, SK entity in EUR. Different bank networks, different card acquirers.
**Pain Point:** Dual-currency operations across two regulatory regimes with different local payment methods require maintaining parallel payment stacks.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API handles multi-currency routing, local acquiring in each market, with unified reporting.
**Best Case:** InDrive — 10 markets, multiple currencies, <8 months to go live.
**Outreach Angle:** "One API for both your CZK and EUR payment flows, with local acquiring in each market."

### Insight #4: Proprietary Pluso Wallet Signals In-House Payment Engineering
**Evidence:** S3A/S4A — Pluso is Tipsport's own e-wallet supporting crypto deposits.
**Pain Point:** Building proprietary payment products in-house ties up engineering resources that could be focused on core betting product.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Orchestration handles the plumbing (routing, failover, reconciliation) while Tipsport keeps focus on differentiated products like Pluso.
**Best Case:** Rappi — zero implementation time, 80% less analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your team built Pluso — impressive. Yuno can handle the payment routing complexity so your engineers stay focused on innovations like that."

### Insight #5: Fraud/Unauthorized Transaction Complaints
**Evidence:** S5 — Casino.guru reports of unauthorized transactions (22,000+ CZK) and deposits not credited.
**Pain Point:** Even isolated fraud incidents erode player trust and create regulatory risk in licensed iGaming.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Real-time fraud monitoring + 1,000+ fraud tool integrations. Rappi case: millisecond detection vs. 5–10 min manual.
**Outreach Angle:** "Real-time payment monitoring that catches anomalies in milliseconds — before they become player complaints or regulatory flags."
