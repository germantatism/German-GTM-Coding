## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Zalando SE is Europe's leading fashion e-commerce platform (ZAL.DE, Frankfurt), generating €10.6B revenue and €15.3B GMV across 25–28 European markets with 51.8M active customers and 251M annual orders (FY2024). Their payment stack is sophisticated but heavily concentrated: Adyen processes European credit cards as primary acquirer and holds **exclusive** partnerships for Cartes Bancaires (France) and Bancontact (Belgium) since November 2024; Klarna handles BNPL across 8 markets; and Zalando's own BaFin-licensed e-money institution (**Zalando Payments GmbH**) manages all payment flows internally — making them effectively their own payment orchestrator. No third-party orchestration platform found. The primary Yuno opportunity sits at three inflection points: **(1)** the About You + SCAYLE Payments acquisition (closed July 2025) creates a payment infrastructure consolidation window; **(2)** the ZEOS B2B platform now onboarding NEXT (24 EU markets) and M&S (21 EU markets) creates multi-merchant payment complexity that internal tools were not built to handle; and **(3)** critical APM gaps in Spain (Bizum), Italy (Satispay/MyBank), and UK (Open Banking) will become a drag as Zalando expands to 28+ markets.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

**Insight #1: About You + SCAYLE Payments Integration = Two BaFin-Licensed EMIs to Merge**
**Evidence**: Section 6 — About You acquisition closed July 2025. SCAYLE Payments GmbH received BaFin EMI license October 2024. Now Zalando has two independent BaFin-licensed payment entities with distinct payment stacks, engineering teams, and PSP relationships.
**Pain Point**: Merging two licensed payment entities across 28+ markets without a unifying orchestration layer risks months of integration engineering, double compliance obligations, and divergent APM coverage.
**Yuno Value Prop**: A single orchestration layer above both entities unifies routing, APM coverage, and reporting — without requiring either EMI to rebuild. New markets from About You's 29-country footprint can go live in weeks.
**Best Success Case**: **InDrive** — unified payment stack across 10 LATAM markets in <8 months.
**Outreach Angle**: "You just closed About You with two BaFin-licensed payment entities and two engineering teams. The fastest path to a unified payment stack isn't rewriting either — it's orchestrating above both."

---

**Insight #2: ZEOS B2B Platform Creates Multi-Merchant Payment Complexity Not Built For**
**Evidence**: Section 6 — NEXT (24 EU markets, Nov 2024) and M&S (21 EU markets, Nov 2025) are onboarding ZEOS. TikTok Shop brands also onboarding.
**Pain Point**: Zalando's internal payment stack (Zalando Payments GmbH + Adyen) was built for Zalando-branded consumer orders. Multi-tenant B2B commerce (NEXT orders, M&S orders, TikTok Shop orders on Zalando logistics) creates a new payment routing problem: different merchants, different PSP preferences, different return/refund policies, different acquirer agreements.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Orchestration layer handles multi-merchant payment routing, allowing ZEOS partners to use their preferred PSPs while Zalando manages the routing in a unified way.
**Best Success Case**: **Rappi** — multi-business super app that used Yuno to unify payment flows across delivery, grocery, and financial services verticals.
**Outreach Angle**: "NEXT and M&S are routing payments through your ZEOS stack across 45+ markets combined. Each brings its own PSP requirements. Yuno orchestrates multi-merchant payment flows so you don't have to build that per partner."

---

**Insight #3: Adyen Exclusivity = Single-Vendor Concentration Risk in Key Markets**
**Evidence**: Section 3 — November 2024 Adyen partnership makes Adyen the exclusive processor for Cartes Bancaires (France, ~8% traffic) and Bancontact (Belgium, ~7% traffic).
**Pain Point**: Exclusive single-vendor dependency for critical local methods means any Adyen outage, pricing renegotiation, or contractual dispute directly impacts conversion in France and Belgium. There is no backup routing for these local methods.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Smart Routing across multiple PSPs provides failover and redundancy. If Adyen has downtime for Cartes Bancaires, a backup route activates instantly.
**Best Success Case**: **Livelo** — +5% approval rate, 50% transaction recovery with Yuno smart routing.
**Outreach Angle**: "Your November 2024 Adyen deal made them exclusive for Cartes Bancaires and Bancontact. That's concentration risk in two of your top-5 EU markets. Yuno adds failover routing without disrupting the Adyen relationship."

---

**Insight #4: Spain Bizum Gap Is a €1B+ Revenue Opportunity**
**Evidence**: Section 4 — Bizum has 28.8M users and ~50% of Spanish mobile bank transfer volume. Amazon ES added it in July 2024. No Zalando ES announcement found.
**Pain Point**: Zalando Spain (est. ~€800M–€1.2B GMV) is not offering the dominant Spanish mobile payment method. Competitors and Amazon are. This is a conversion drag on the #6 traffic market.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Bizum activation via Yuno's no-code PSP enablement — weeks not months.
**Best Success Case**: **Reserva** — +4% approval rate in <3 months from adding localized payment methods.
**Outreach Angle**: "Amazon added Bizum in Spain in July 2024. Zalando still doesn't have it. That's a checkout conversion gap in a market with 28.8M Bizum users. Yuno can activate it in weeks."

---

**Insight #5: Open Banking / Pay by Bank Is the Next Logical UK Gap**
**Evidence**: Section 4 — UK Open Banking reached 351M payments in 2025. Farfetch (direct competitor) launched Pay by Bank via Volt in UK, Germany, and Netherlands in 2024. No Zalando UK announcement found.
**Pain Point**: UK fashion e-commerce is intensely competitive. Farfetch and others are already offering Open Banking. Zalando UK (est. ~€500M+ GMV) is missing this payment method while a competitor activates it.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Open Banking / Pay by Bank activation across UK via single API integration — no separate bilateral partnerships required.
**Best Success Case**: **InDrive** — new market live in weeks with Yuno. Open Banking is a new "market" for Zalando UK.
**Outreach Angle**: "Farfetch launched Pay by Bank in the UK in 2024. With 351M UK Open Banking payments annually, Zalando.co.uk not offering it is a growing gap. We can activate it in weeks."
