# SDR Research Brief — Voodoo
*Yuno Payment Orchestrator · Framework v8.0 · Research date 2026-06-22*

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Voodoo SAS (Paris) is the world's #3 mobile games publisher (€623M revenue / +20% YoY / €135M EBITDA in 2024, ~150M MAU, 950M downloads in 2024) that has pivoted from 100% ad-funded hyper-casual to **78% hybrid-casual/other (IAP-heavy)** and owns the social app **BeReal** (acquired June 2024, ~€500M; ~40M MAU). The single most relevant payments fact: **Voodoo already went live with Stripe in June 2025 to pull US iOS purchases OFF Apple's IAP rails into an external Stripe Checkout web page, with Stripe as Merchant of Record** ("save up to 90% in fees"). This makes Voodoo a *confirmed active buyer* of external/web checkout — not a hypothetical. **The Yuno opportunity is orchestration of that net-new web-billing surface across geographies and rails:** today it is Stripe-only, US-only, on the highest-margin revenue stream, with single-PSP concentration risk and no second processor or failover.

> ⚠️ NOTE: In the Yuno ICP list this account is tagged **NO OPP** (mobile/IAP-dominated). The Stripe web-checkout move is what reopens it — pitch the orchestration layer on the external-purchase rail, not the IAP rail.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

**Insight #1: Confirmed external-checkout buyer, but Stripe-only / US-only**
Evidence: §3A, §7 — Voodoo built a Stripe MoR web checkout for US iOS in June 2025. | Pain: single PSP on the highest-margin revenue stream; no failover, no second acquirer, no local-APM routing outside the US. | Yuno value: one orchestration layer to add a backup PSP, smart-route, and recover failed web payments (+7% approval, 50% recovery). | Best case: **InDrive** (10 LATAM markets <8 months). | Angle: "You've already proven the web-checkout model with Stripe in the US — the next 90% of fee savings and approval uplift comes from doing it across India/Japan/EU on multiple rails, not one."

**Insight #2: Geographic expansion of web billing = local-APM gap**
Evidence: §1, §4B — India 8% web, BeReal Japan #1; no local APMs (UPI, PayPay, Konbini) confirmed. | Pain: Stripe-centric, EUR-settled, card-first checkout underperforms in APM-led markets. | Yuno value: 1,000+ methods, local APMs live in weeks, cheaper local acquiring. | Best case: **InDrive / Rappi**. | Angle: card-only web checkout leaves UPI/PayPay conversion on the table.

**Insight #3: New transactional surfaces — Jamble + BeReal ad billing**
Evidence: §6 — Jamble live-shopping (US growth) + BeReal RealBrands advertiser billing (200+ advertisers). | Pain: net-new payment flows (consumer commerce + B2B media spend) with no orchestration layer. | Yuno value: one API across consumer + B2B billing, multi-region settlement. | Best case: **Rappi** (commerce orchestration). | Angle: live-shopping and advertiser billing are real card flows that don't belong on IAP rails.

**Insight #4: Zero payment-data visibility today**
Evidence: §5 — Voodoo's own terms admit no access to transactions; refunds offloaded to Apple/Google. | Pain: can't optimize approval, retry, or disputes. | Yuno value: real-time monitors (Rappi: ms detection vs 5-10 min manual), full data ownership. | Angle: own the customer-payment relationship the app stores currently hold.
