# Fluz — SDR Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-30 | **Analyst:** Claude | **Framework:** v8.0

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Fluz (fluz.app) is a New York-based cashback and rewards platform founded in 2018 that enables consumers to earn cashback on gift card purchases at 4,000+ brands and via single-use virtual Mastercards. In February 2026, Fluz launched **Fluz Platform**, pivoting into B2B embedded payments infrastructure — managing pay-ins, wallets, controlled spend, rewards, and disbursements for software companies. The company claims **$5B+ in lifetime processed payments** with ~91 employees across 4 continents, yet has only $533K in disclosed institutional funding. No third-party payment orchestrator was detected — Fluz appears to run proprietary payment rails with Plaid for ACH connectivity and Mastercard-licensed virtual cards through partner banks. The Yuno opportunity lies in the B2B platform scaling: as Fluz Platform onboards enterprise clients across multiple markets, they will need multi-PSP routing, local acquiring, and payment orchestration to optimize approval rates and expand geographically.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: B2B Platform Scaling Needs Multi-PSP Infrastructure
**Evidence:** S6 — Fluz Platform launched Feb 2026 targeting fintechs, marketplaces, and financial services firms with embedded payments.
**Pain Point:** As Fluz Platform onboards enterprise clients operating in multiple markets, a single-PSP or proprietary-only stack won't scale for local acquiring, currency optimization, and regulatory compliance across geographies.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API to 1,000+ payment methods across 200+ countries. No-code PSP enablement lets Fluz Platform offer its enterprise clients localized payment coverage without building each integration.
**Best Case:** Yuno becomes the orchestration layer behind Fluz Platform, enabling Fluz to offer multi-market payment coverage to its B2B clients.
**Outreach Angle:** "As Fluz Platform scales beyond the US, orchestrating local PSPs per market will be critical. Yuno can power that layer so your team focuses on the product, not payment integrations."

### Insight #2: Payment Method Lock Complaints Signal Risk Management Gaps
**Evidence:** S5 — Recurring Trustpilot/BBB complaints about payment methods being locked without explanation, causing user churn.
**Pain Point:** Aggressive or opaque risk rules suggest limited routing options. Users are blocked from transacting with no recourse.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing can optimize transaction approval by routing through the PSP most likely to approve. Real-time monitoring (like Rappi: ms-level detection vs. 5–10 min manually) gives operational visibility into decline patterns.
**Best Case:** Reduce false declines by 50%+ through transaction recovery and smart routing, directly improving user retention and cashback revenue.
**Outreach Angle:** "I noticed Fluz users flagging payment lockouts on Trustpilot. InDrive solved a similar problem — 90% approval rates across 10 LATAM markets with Yuno's Smart Routing."

### Insight #3: No Orchestrator Detected — Greenfield Opportunity
**Evidence:** S3B — No evidence of Spreedly, Primer, Gr4vy, or any third-party orchestrator. Fluz runs proprietary payment rails.
**Pain Point:** Proprietary infrastructure is expensive to maintain and limits flexibility. Adding new PSPs, markets, or payment methods requires custom development.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno replaces months of integration work with a single API. New market live in weeks with no-code PSP enablement.
**Best Case:** Fluz adopts Yuno as the orchestration layer for both consumer app and B2B platform, reducing engineering overhead and accelerating geographic expansion.
**Outreach Angle:** "Building your own payment stack is impressive, but orchestrating multiple PSPs for enterprise clients across markets is a different challenge. That's exactly what Yuno does."

### Insight #4: Remote Partnership Signals Workforce Payments Expansion
**Evidence:** S6 — Partnership with Remote for contractor payments optimization.
**Pain Point:** Cross-border contractor payouts require local rails, currency conversion, and compliance — pain points that multiply per market.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's 200+ country coverage and local acquiring can optimize payout costs and speed. Smart Routing ensures the best path per transaction.
**Best Case:** Yuno powers the payout rails for Fluz Platform's workforce/contractor payment use case.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your Remote partnership is smart — contractor payments across borders is exactly where local acquiring and smart routing drive cost savings."

### Insight #5: Cashback Tier Structure Creates PSP Cost Sensitivity
**Evidence:** S4A — Cashback rates vary by -1% to -3% depending on funding source, suggesting Fluz absorbs processing costs.
**Pain Point:** Credit card and PayPal transactions cost Fluz 3% more in cashback, directly impacting margins. Optimizing processing costs per payment method is critical to unit economics.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing can optimize for cost by routing transactions through the lowest-cost acquirer per payment method and card type.
**Best Case:** Even 0.5% reduction in processing costs across $5B+ lifetime volume = $25M+ in savings.
**Outreach Angle:** "When your cashback tiers eat 1–3% per transaction, every basis point in processing cost matters. Yuno's Smart Routing optimizes for the lowest-cost acquirer per transaction."
