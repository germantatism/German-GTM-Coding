## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SEGA is a global gaming conglomerate (~$2.9B revenue) with 48+ subsidiaries across 20+ countries, operating across console/PC gaming, mobile F2P, merchandise e-commerce, and — critically — a fast-growing iGaming division through its acquisitions of GAN Limited (B2B sports betting/casino platform, May 2025) and Stakelogic (online gambling, 2024). Their direct payment infrastructure is fragmented: Shopify Payments for merchandise, Xsolla for digital game stores, and no confirmed payment orchestrator in place. The strongest Yuno opportunity lies in the iGaming subsidiaries (GAN + Stakelogic), which require multi-PSP redundancy, local payment methods, and smart routing across regulated jurisdictions — followed by D2C digital storefront expansion to reduce platform fees.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

**Insight #1: iGaming Expansion Creates Urgent Multi-PSP Orchestration Need**
**Evidence**: Section 6 — GAN acquisition ($107.6M, May 2025) + Stakelogic acquisition (~EUR 125M, 2024). Two B2B iGaming platforms now under SEGA Sammy umbrella.
**Pain Point**: Regulated gambling requires multi-PSP redundancy, local payment methods per jurisdiction, smart routing, KYC/compliance tooling, and instant payouts — across every licensed market. Integrating two separate iGaming platforms (GAN GameSTACK + Stakelogic) creates payment stack fragmentation.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Single orchestration layer across both iGaming platforms — smart routing for optimal approval rates, unified compliance, one integration for all PSPs and local methods across regulated markets.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — 10 LATAM markets in <8 months, 90% approval rate — resonates because iGaming also requires rapid multi-market deployment with local payment support.
**Outreach Angle**: "SEGA Sammy now operates two separate iGaming platforms (GAN + Stakelogic) across multiple regulated jurisdictions — payment orchestration could unify PSP management and optimize approval rates across both, similar to how we helped InDrive launch across 10 markets."

**Insight #2: Fragmented Payment Stack Across 4+ Separate Systems**
**Evidence**: Section 3 — Shopify Payments (merch), Xsolla (digital games), GAN (iGaming), Stakelogic (iGaming), plus platform partners (Steam, App Store, etc.)
**Pain Point**: No unified payment visibility or analytics across business lines. Each division operates its own payment stack independently, preventing cross-business optimization and creating reconciliation overhead.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Unified payment orchestration dashboard — single view across all business lines, real-time monitoring, automated reconciliation.
**Best Success Case**: Rappi — zero implementation time for new providers, 80% reduction in analyst resolution time — resonates because SEGA needs consolidated payment operations.
**Outreach Angle**: "With Shopify, Xsolla, GAN, and Stakelogic all running separate payment stacks, SEGA likely lacks unified payment visibility — we help companies like Rappi consolidate and cut analyst resolution time by 80%."

**Insight #3: 91% Profit Decline Creates Conversion Optimization Urgency**
**Evidence**: Section 6/7 — H1 FY2026 net profit collapsed 91% YoY to JPY 2.75B; missed FY2026 revenue projection; F2P and full game sales both underperformed.
**Pain Point**: When profits are collapsing, every basis point of payment conversion matters. Optimizing approval rates and reducing transaction failures is low-hanging fruit.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Smart routing delivers up to +7% approval rate uplift; 50% transaction recovery on failed payments.
**Best Success Case**: Livelo — +5% approval rate, 50% transaction recovery — directly addresses revenue leakage.
**Outreach Angle**: "With SEGA Sammy's H1 profits down 91%, optimizing payment conversion is one of the fastest levers — we've delivered +5% approval uplift and 50% recovery on failed transactions for similar companies."

**Insight #4: D2C Web Stores Signal Platform Fee Reduction Strategy**
**Evidence**: Section 8 — SEGA operates dedicated web stores (P5X, Colorful Stage) using Xsolla to sell in-game currency directly, bypassing App Store/Google Play 30% commission.
**Pain Point**: D2C stores need robust multi-method, multi-geography payment support to compete with the frictionless platform store checkout experience. Currently limited to Xsolla as single provider.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Unified Checkout Builder per region, 1,000+ payment methods, no-code PSP enablement — makes D2C checkout as smooth as platform stores.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — rapid market expansion with localized payment methods.
**Outreach Angle**: "SEGA's D2C game stores are a smart move to reduce platform fees — orchestrating multiple PSPs with local methods per market can maximize conversion and make the D2C experience competitive with App Store/Steam checkout."

**Insight #5: No Geo-Adaptive Checkout on Merchandise Store**
**Evidence**: Section 4/8 — shop.sega.com shows same payment methods globally. No regional APM adaptation. Only Klarna for BNPL (no Affirm, Afterpay).
**Pain Point**: Global gaming fans visiting from markets where local payment methods dominate (Brazil, Germany, Netherlands, India) face a limited checkout that may reduce conversion.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Checkout Builder with automatic geo-adaptive APM display — show PIX in Brazil, iDEAL in Netherlands, UPI in India, etc.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — localized payment methods across multiple markets drove 90% approval rates.
**Outreach Angle**: "SEGA's global merch store currently shows the same payment options everywhere — geo-adaptive checkout with local methods per market could significantly lift conversion."
