## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Dream Games is a Turkish mobile gaming studio valued at $5B (CVC Capital Partners, May 2025) with ~$2B+ annual revenue across two titles — Royal Match (~$1.6B/yr) and Royal Kingdom (~$300M first year). The company monetizes 100% through in-app purchases with zero ad revenue, processing all payments through Apple App Store, Google Play, Samsung Galaxy Store, Amazon Appstore, and Huawei AppGallery. **Dream Games currently has NO direct payment processing, no web store, and no PSP relationships** — making them a non-traditional Yuno prospect today. However, at ~$2B/yr revenue, they are potentially losing $300M–$600M annually in app store commissions (15–30%), and the industry trend toward D2C web shops post-April 2025 court ruling creates a significant future opportunity if Dream Games decides to process payments directly.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

> **IMPORTANT CONTEXT:** Dream Games is currently a non-traditional prospect for Yuno. They have no direct payment processing today. All outreach angles are forward-looking — contingent on Dream Games pursuing a D2C web store strategy to bypass app store commissions.

**Insight #1: D2C Web Store Opportunity — $300M–$600M Annual Commission Savings**
**Evidence**: Section 3 — All $2B+ annual revenue processed through app stores at 15–30% commission. Section 4 — No web store exists. Industry trend toward D2C web shops accelerating post-April 2025 court ruling.
**Pain Point**: Dream Games is paying an estimated $300M–$600M/year in app store commissions. Competitors (HoYoverse) already operate D2C web stores.
**Yuno Value Prop**: If Dream Games builds a D2C web shop, Yuno's payment orchestration (1,000+ payment methods, 200+ countries) would enable global payment processing from day one with smart routing, local acquiring, and region-specific APMs.
**Best Success Case**: InDrive — 10 markets in <8 months, 90% approval rate. Resonates because Dream Games would need rapid global payment coverage.
**Outreach Angle**: "At $2B+/yr through app stores, a D2C web shop could save $300M+ annually. Yuno could power the payment infrastructure for that transition."

**Insight #2: 100% IAP Revenue = Maximum Payment Sensitivity**
**Evidence**: Section 7 — Zero ad revenue. Every dollar flows through payments.
**Pain Point**: Every payment decline is lost revenue with no ad revenue buffer. At this scale, even small approval rate improvements = tens of millions.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Smart Routing delivers up to +7% approval rate uplift. On $2B revenue, 1% improvement = $20M.
**Best Success Case**: Livelo — +5% approval rate, 50% transaction recovery.
**Outreach Angle**: "With 100% IAP monetization, every payment decline is lost revenue. At your scale, even small approval rate improvements translate to tens of millions."

**Insight #3: Multi-Title Expansion Increases Complexity**
**Evidence**: Section 6 — Royal Kingdom launched Nov 2024, already $300M+ first year.
**Pain Point**: Managing payment performance across multiple titles, multiple app stores, 190+ countries.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Unified payment dashboard, real-time monitoring, single integration.
**Best Success Case**: Rappi — 80% reduction in analyst resolution time.
**Outreach Angle**: "As Royal Kingdom scales alongside Royal Match, unified payment intelligence could optimize pricing and approval rates by market."

**Insight #4: PE-Backed Growth Mandate**
**Evidence**: Section 6 — CVC Capital Partners majority stake, mandate to "accelerate global expansion."
**Pain Point**: PE firms optimize for margin. App store commissions are the single largest cost center.
**Yuno Value Prop**: Payment orchestration enables commission savings through D2C channels.
**Outreach Angle**: "Post-CVC acquisition, payment infrastructure optimization — especially D2C — is the highest-impact lever at your revenue scale."
