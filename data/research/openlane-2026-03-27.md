## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**OPENLANE, Inc.** (NYSE: OPLN, formerly KAR Global) is a leading digital marketplace for wholesale vehicle remarketing, operating across the **U.S., Canada, UK, and Continental Europe**. The company processes **~$15B+ in wholesale vehicle GMV annually** with 2025 revenue of **$1,935M** (+8% YoY). Their payment stack is bank-rails-only — **ACH via Plaid, wire transfers through Wells Fargo, and proprietary floorplan financing through their subsidiary AFC** — with no traditional PSP or payment orchestrator detected. The primary Yuno opportunity centers on their **European cross-border payment friction** (compliance-based payment rejections, misdirected payments), the upcoming **$7–10B off-lease GMV inflection in Q2 2026**, and the potential to modernize payment infrastructure as **70% of the $15B wholesale market migrates from offline to digital**.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: European Cross-Border Payment Friction Creates Immediate Pain
**Evidence:** [S5] Trustpilot EU complaints — compliance-based payment rejections (buyer must pay from company bank account in home country); misdirected payments to wrong bank accounts
**Pain Point:** EU buyers in Romania, Poland, Portugal face friction with strict country-matching bank transfer requirements, limiting cross-border purchasing
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's local acquiring in 200+ countries could enable local payment acceptance in each EU market, eliminating country-matching friction. Smart routing ensures payments are processed through optimal local rails.
**Best Case:** Rappi — zero implementation time, 80% less analyst resolution time for payment operations
**Outreach Angle:** "Your EU buyers are getting payment rejections because of country-matching restrictions. Yuno enables local payment acceptance in every European market without requiring local bank accounts."

### Insight #2: $7–10B Off-Lease GMV Inflection Demands Scalable Payment Infrastructure
**Evidence:** [S6, S7] Q2 2026 off-lease volume inflection expected to generate $7–10B in additional GMV; 70% of $15B wholesale market still offline
**Pain Point:** Current bank-rails-only infrastructure (ACH, wire, EFT) may not scale efficiently to handle the massive volume increase, especially across multiple markets
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's single API could process payments across all markets with smart routing for optimal approval rates. New market live in weeks, no-code PSP enablement.
**Best Case:** InDrive — 10 LATAM markets in <8 months, 90% approval rates
**Outreach Angle:** "With $7–10B in new off-lease volume hitting Q2 2026, how are you scaling payment infrastructure across US, Canada, and Europe from a single integration?"

### Insight #3: No Payment Orchestrator = Single Points of Failure
**Evidence:** [S3] No orchestrator detected; payments route through Wells Fargo (wire), Plaid (ACH), and individual bank transfer rails per market
**Pain Point:** Without orchestration, each market requires separate payment integration and maintenance. No failover, no smart routing, no unified reconciliation.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno provides single-API orchestration across all payment methods and markets, with built-in failover and smart routing (+7% approval uplift).
**Best Case:** Livelo — +5% approval, 50% recovery
**Outreach Angle:** "You're processing billions in GMV across 4+ markets with separate payment rails in each. One API could unify your ACH, wire, EFT, and bank transfers with smart routing and real-time monitoring."

### Insight #4: Integrated Finance Arm (AFC) Creates Unique Orchestration Opportunity
**Evidence:** [S3, S7] AFC manages $2.4B in receivables; nearly half of AFC dealers now transact on marketplace; 30+ floorplan providers accepted
**Pain Point:** Managing 30+ floorplan financing providers alongside ACH, wire, and bank transfer creates operational complexity. No unified view of transaction flows.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's real-time monitoring dashboard could provide unified visibility across all payment methods and financing providers, similar to Rappi's ms-level detection vs. 5–10 min manual monitoring.
**Best Case:** Rappi — real-time monitoring, 80% less analyst resolution time
**Outreach Angle:** "With 30+ floorplan providers and multiple payment rails, you're managing complexity that a single orchestration layer could simplify while adding real-time monitoring."

### Insight #5: Mobile Payment Experience Degradation
**Evidence:** [S5, S8] Post-October 2025 app update causing crashes and slow performance on Android; app supports in-app payments
**Pain Point:** Mobile payment flow disruptions directly impact transaction completion rates for dealers bidding and paying on-the-go
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's mobile-optimized SDKs provide stable, consistent payment experiences with built-in retry logic and transaction recovery (50% transaction recovery rate)
**Best Case:** Reserva — +4% approval in <3 months
**Outreach Angle:** "Your dealers are reporting payment-flow issues on mobile after recent updates. A dedicated payment SDK could decouple payment stability from app releases."
