## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Bumble Inc. is a publicly traded (NASDAQ: BMBL) online dating company operating Bumble and Badoo across 150+ countries, generating ~$1.07B in FY2024 revenue with 3.6M paying subscribers. The company is undergoing a major transformation: revenue declining (-10% YoY in Q3 2025), paying users down 16%, a full C-suite refresh in 2025, and an AI-first cloud-native platform rebuild targeted for mid-2026. Critically, Bumble is aggressively pursuing **direct web billing** to bypass Apple/Google app store commissions (15-30%), making payment orchestration increasingly relevant. Bumble operates dedicated payment entities ("Social Online Payments Limited" in Ireland and "Social Online Payments LLC" in Delaware) but no payment orchestrator was identified — a clear Yuno opportunity during their platform rebuild.
