# SDR Research Brief — SendOwl (sendowl.com)
**Date:** 2026-03-25 | **Analyst:** Yuno Payments Intelligence

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SendOwl is a digital product selling platform (trading as "Concept Den Ltd" in the UK) that enables creators and businesses to sell e-books, courses, music, memberships, subscriptions, and other digital goods anywhere online. Founded in 2010 by George Palmer (London), acquired in 2020 by Matt Plotke (ex-Stripe, ex-LinkedIn). The company has raised $13.5M in seed funding (Defy.vc, Stripe, Alumni Ventures, TheGP, Authentic Ventures) and claims to have facilitated ~$2B in digital goods transactions for tens of thousands of merchants. SendOwl does NOT process payments itself -- it delegates all payment processing to **Stripe**, **PayPal**, and **BitPay**, meaning it acts as a commerce/delivery layer, not a PSP. With ~830K monthly visits (9.978M annual), 42% US traffic, and flat-fee SaaS pricing ($39-$299+/mo), SendOwl is a potential Yuno partner/integration target rather than a direct PSP customer -- unless they decide to internalize payment orchestration to offer merchants better rates/routing.

- Alipay

**Key Architecture Insight:** SendOwl explicitly states: "SendOwl doesn't process any payments, place a hold on your funds, or take a percentage of the payment. Your payment gateway handles payment, and SendOwl delivers the product." This means SendOwl is a commerce/fulfillment layer sitting on top of PSPs -- merchants bring their own Stripe/PayPal/BitPay accounts. SendOwl has NO payment orchestration layer.

**Stripe is a Strategic Investor:** Stripe participated in SendOwl's $4.5M seed round (Dec 2021). This makes Stripe both a payment partner and an equity holder, which may create resistance to adding alternative PSPs or an orchestration layer.

## SECTION 4 -- PCI DSS

**No PCI DSS certification found for SendOwl/Concept Den Ltd.**

This is consistent with their architecture: SendOwl does not process, store, or transmit cardholder data. All payment processing is delegated to Stripe, PayPal, and BitPay, which are each PCI DSS Level 1 certified. SendOwl merchants' checkout flows redirect to or embed these PSPs' hosted payment forms.

SendOwl's website does host third-party PCI DSS training courses for sale (using their own platform), but this is unrelated to their own compliance status.

Sources: [PCI SSC](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/) | [SendOwl PCI courses page](https://www.sendowl.com/s/pci-dss)

## SECTION 6 -- What Is SendOwl?

**Business Model:** SaaS platform for selling digital products. SendOwl provides the commerce infrastructure (checkout pages, product delivery, subscription management, affiliate programs, analytics) while merchants use their own payment processor accounts (Stripe, PayPal, BitPay). SendOwl earns revenue from flat monthly subscriptions, not transaction fees.

**Industry Classification:**
- SIC 62012: Business and domestic software development
- Category: E-commerce SaaS / Digital Commerce Platform / Creator Economy Infrastructure
- Competitors: Gumroad, Payhip, Sellfy, Podia, Lemon Squeezy, Paddle (partial overlap)

**What They Sell (on behalf of merchants):**
- E-books, PDFs, audiobooks
- Online courses, coaching sessions
- Music, videos, podcasts
- Software, license keys
- Templates, graphic design files
- Memberships & subscriptions
- Live remote events
- NFT pre-sales

**Key Differentiators:**
- No transaction fees (flat SaaS pricing)
- Embeddable anywhere via payment links (no storefront required)
- Deep Shopify integration (Shopify App Store)
- PDF stamping & document security
- Drip content delivery
- Affiliate program management built-in

**Founding Timeline:**
- 2010-2011: George Palmer builds SendOwl as a side project while freelancing in Ruby on Rails (London)
- 2018: Palmer resigns as director; international directors appointed (Canadian, Australian, Irish)
- 2020: Matt Plotke (ex-Stripe Head of Growth, ex-LinkedIn) acquires the company and becomes CEO/Director
- 2021: $4.5M seed from Defy.vc + Stripe
- 2023: $9M seed from TheGP + Alumni Ventures + Authentic Ventures
- Present: Remote-first company, employees across 3 continents, 50+ countries served
