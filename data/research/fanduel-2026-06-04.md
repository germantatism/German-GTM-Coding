## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

FanDuel is the #1 US online sportsbook/iGaming operator (≈$7B US revenue FY2025), now a **100%-owned subsidiary of Flutter Entertainment** (NYSE/LSE: FLUT) as of July 31, 2025. It runs a **fragmented multi-rail US payment stack** — card acquiring (Nuvei, strongest signal), online banking (Trustly), cash-at-retail (PayNearMe), prepaid (Sightline/Play+), plus PayPal, Venmo, Apple Pay and debit — with **no public evidence of a third-party orchestrator** (likely in-house at Flutter scale). The standout Yuno opportunities: (1) FanDuel **eliminated all US credit-card deposits effective March 2, 2026**, concentrating volume onto debit/bank rails that suffer issuer "gambling-block" declines — a textbook smart-routing / decline-recovery angle; (2) it is **actively building payments/wallet capability across new verticals** (two open *Lead PM, Payments* roles, one dedicated to the brand-new FanDuel Predicts prediction-market product) — a greenfield orchestration window; (3) persistent **withdrawal/payout-reliability complaints**. Caveat: FanDuel is essentially single-country (US + Ontario), so Yuno's classic cross-border thesis is weaker here — lead with auth-rate recovery, payout reliability, and multi-vertical/multi-state orchestration instead.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

**Insight #1: Credit-card removal concentrates volume onto decline-prone debit/bank rails**
Evidence: S39 (credit cards gone Mar 2, 2026) + S25 (deposit declines from issuer gambling-blocks). Pain Point: Legitimate deposits soft-declined by banks; now nowhere to fall back to since credit is gone → lost first-deposit conversion. Yuno Value Prop: Smart Routing (+7% approval) + **50% transaction recovery** + intelligent retry/cascading across debit, Trustly, PayPal/Venmo. Best Case: **InDrive** (90% approval, 4.5% recovery). Outreach Angle: *"With credit cards off the menu as of March, every declined debit/bank deposit is now a lost first wager — that's exactly the recovery layer Yuno adds on top of Nuvei/Trustly without re-integrating."*

**Insight #2: New-vertical wallet build (FanDuel Predicts) = greenfield orchestration window**
Evidence: S29/S38 (Predicts launch + dedicated *Lead PM Payments, Predictions* hiring deposits/withdrawals/wallet). Pain Point: Standing up payments for a brand-new CFTC product across many states, fast. Yuno Value Prop: New market/vertical live in **weeks**, no-code PSP enablement, one API across sportsbook/casino/racing/DFS/Predicts. Best Case: **Rappi** (zero implementation time). Outreach Angle: *"You're staffing payments PMs specifically for Predicts — Yuno lets a new vertical reuse one orchestration layer instead of rebuilding the wallet rail-by-rail."*

**Insight #3: Payout/withdrawal reliability is a live complaint cluster**
Evidence: S23/S24 (money "sent" but not received; check-payout loss; holds). Pain Point: Withdrawal failures erode trust and trigger support load. Yuno Value Prop: Real-time payout monitors (Rappi: ms detection vs 5–10 min manual) + multi-rail payout routing/fallback. Best Case: **Rappi** (80% less analyst resolution time). Outreach Angle: *"The withdrawal complaints on BBB/Trustpilot are a routing/visibility problem Yuno's real-time monitors are built for."*

**Insight #4: Multi-rail, no orchestrator, multi-vertical, 24-state fragmentation**
Evidence: S15–S18 (5+ rails) + S3B (no orchestrator) + per-state method logic. Pain Point: Each rail integrated point-to-point; method availability hard-coded per state. Yuno Value Prop: Single orchestration layer, no-code rail toggling per state/vertical. Best Case: **Livelo** (+5% approval, 50% recovery). Outreach Angle: *"Adding/swapping a processor per state shouldn't need an eng sprint."*
