## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Poki B.V. is an Amsterdam-based free-to-play browser gaming platform serving 100M+ monthly active users across 1,500+ games from 600+ developers. The platform is 100% ad-supported — there is no consumer checkout, no subscription tier, no in-app purchases, and no payment processing of any kind. Poki has zero digital payment transactions, making it a clear anti-ICP for Yuno's payment orchestration platform. The only payment flow is B2B developer revenue-share payouts, which falls outside Yuno's core product.

> VERDICT: ANTI-ICP — DO NOT PURSUE
> Poki has no payments complexity. Their business is advertising, not commerce. No consumer payment flow exists.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights & Outreach Angles

> This section is intentionally abbreviated because Poki is an anti-ICP.

**Insight #1: Zero Consumer Payment Flow**
Evidence: Sections 3, 4 — No PSP, no checkout, no payment methods of any kind.
Assessment: Poki's entire business model is advertising. They have zero payment transactions to optimize, route, or orchestrate. Yuno has no value proposition for their current business.

**Insight #2: Developer Payouts — Tangential Opportunity**
Evidence: Section 6 — 600+ developers receiving ad revenue share globally.
Pain Point: Cross-border developer disbursements at scale.
Assessment: This is a B2B payout/disbursement use case, not Yuno's core payment orchestration product. Companies like Tipalti, Payoneer, or Hyperwallet are better fits.

**Insight #3: Future Watch — Non-Ad Revenue Experimentation**
Evidence: Section 6 — Co-founder mentioned "experimenting with non-ad revenue models."
Assessment: If Poki ever launches a premium subscription or in-game purchases, they would need a payment stack. This is speculative and no timeline exists. Monitor only.

Recommendation: Do not pursue. Revisit only if Poki announces a paid product.
