# SDR Research Brief — Olyn
**Date:** 2026-03-27 | **Analyst:** Payments Intelligence (Yuno)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Olyn (olyn.com) is an early-stage media distribution platform — "Shopify for filmmakers" — headquartered in San Francisco and Barcelona. The company raised ~$2.8M in pre-seed funding (Hard Yaka) and launched in January 2025, securing a Beatles biopic as a marquee deal. Their payment stack runs on **Shift4 Payments** and **Checkout.com** (both confirmed via Terms of Service), with no evidence of a payment orchestrator. The dual-PSP setup, single global domain without localized APMs, and ambitions for cross-border creator payouts via stablecoins present a clear Yuno opportunity — particularly around smart routing between their two PSPs and enabling local payment methods as they scale internationally.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Dual-PSP Without Orchestration = Routing Opportunity
**Evidence:** Section 3 — Shift4 + Checkout.com both named in ToS, no orchestrator detected.
**Pain Point:** Without smart routing, Olyn cannot dynamically route transactions to the PSP with higher approval rates or lower fees for a given market/BIN.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing between Shift4 and Checkout.com could yield +7% approval uplift and optimize processing costs across both PSPs.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates across 10 LATAM markets using Yuno's orchestration.
**Outreach Angle:** "You're already running two PSPs — Yuno can make them work together intelligently instead of in silos."

### Insight #2: Global Ambitions with No Local APMs
**Evidence:** Section 4 — Only cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay confirmed. No local payment methods (PIX, OXXO, UPI, SOFORT, etc.) despite claiming global card acceptance.
**Pain Point:** As Olyn scales internationally (they accept cards from "almost any bank in the world"), they'll face declining approval rates in markets where local APMs dominate.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Single API to enable 1,000+ local payment methods across 200+ countries, no-code PSP enablement.
**Best Case:** Reserva achieved +4% approval uplift in <3 months with Yuno.
**Outreach Angle:** "As filmmakers bring audiences from Brazil, Mexico, India — are you ready with PIX, OXXO, UPI? Yuno plugs those in without engineering lift."

### Insight #3: Creator Payout Complexity
**Evidence:** Section 7 — Olyn Substack discusses instant global payouts, split revenue, and interest in Circle's CPN/stablecoins.
**Pain Point:** Cross-border creator payouts are complex — FX, compliance, settlement timing. They're actively exploring stablecoins as a solution, which signals current payout friction.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno's cross-border payout capabilities and multi-currency settlement could simplify their creator payout infrastructure.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved zero implementation time with real-time monitoring.
**Outreach Angle:** "Paying creators in 200+ countries is the hard part — Yuno's already solved that for platforms like Rappi."

### Insight #4: Early-Stage = Right Time to Build on Orchestration
**Evidence:** Section 6 — Launched Jan 2025, $2.8M raised, actively scaling content and distribution.
**Pain Point:** Building payment infrastructure from scratch is expensive. They're at the inflection point where payment complexity grows faster than their engineering team.
**Yuno Value Prop:** No-code PSP enablement, live in new markets in weeks. Avoids building and maintaining payment routing logic in-house.
**Best Case:** InDrive went live in 10 LATAM markets in <8 months.
**Outreach Angle:** "You're building the Shopify for filmmakers — let Yuno be your Shopify for payments."
