## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Fanatics is the world's largest licensed sports merchandise platform — $8.1B revenue in 2024 across three distinct business lines (Commerce $6.2B, Collectibles $1.6B, Sportsbook $300M) — operating in 180+ countries with offices in the US, UK, Italy, Japan, and India. Their payment stack is notably fragmented: Paysafe confirmed for the sportsbook, Stripe inferred (not confirmed) for Collectibles, and the Commerce PSP (their largest business at 77% of revenue) is entirely unknown from public sources. No payment orchestration layer has been identified across any business unit. Combined with 53% 1-star Trustpilot reviews citing checkout errors and refund failures, a rapid international expansion (Italy 2024, Voggt EU 2024, Rugby Australia, ICC Cricket), and a closed-loop loyalty ecosystem (Fanatics ONE) that will require sophisticated payment plumbing — Fanatics is a high-conviction Yuno target for orchestration, APM expansion, and cross-business payment unification.

**Best Success Case:** Livelo — similar high-volume, loyalty-driven commerce business where transaction recovery directly impacted revenue.
**Outreach Angle:** "Fanatics processes $6.2B in merchandise GMV — and their Commerce PSP isn't even public. That either means a single-PSP dependency with no failover, or infrastructure that hasn't been benchmarked in years. We can close that gap without a full migration."
