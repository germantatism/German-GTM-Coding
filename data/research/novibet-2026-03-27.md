# Novibet — SDR Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-27 | **Analyst:** Yuno Payments Intelligence

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Novibet is a Greek-founded online sports betting and casino operator (parent: Logflex MT Holding Limited, Malta) operating across 9+ regulated markets including Greece, Brazil, Ireland, Mexico, Chile, Ecuador, Cyprus, Canada (Ontario), and New Zealand, with 1,000+ employees. **Nuvei is the confirmed PSP** for card processing and alternative payments globally — no payment orchestration platform was found. The Allwyn acquisition (EUR 217M+ deal for 51% stake) collapsed in March 2026 due to Greek competition authority objections, leaving Novibet independent and potentially in a strategic reset moment. With significant withdrawal complaints, multi-market payment complexity (PIX in Brazil, SEPA in Europe, local methods in LatAm), and active expansion into North America, Novibet is a strong candidate for payment orchestration.

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

### Insight #1: Single-PSP Dependency on Nuvei

**Evidence:** Section 3 — Nuvei confirmed as global PSP for card processing and APMs. No other PSP confirmed.
**Pain Point:** Single-PSP dependency creates risk: if Nuvei experiences downtime, Novibet loses all card processing capability across 9+ markets. No fallback routing.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing across multiple PSPs → +7% approval uplift, 50% transaction recovery. Add backup PSPs without engineering effort via no-code enablement.
**Best Case:** InDrive achieved 90% approval rates and 4.5% recovery across 10 LATAM markets.
**Outreach Angle:** "With Nuvei as your single global processor, a single outage affects every market. Yuno's orchestration layer adds failover routing and local acquirers where they outperform cross-border — no migration needed."

### Insight #2: Withdrawal Friction = Revenue & Trust Erosion

**Evidence:** Section 5 — HIGH-frequency complaints about declined withdrawals, delayed payouts, PayPal failures across AskGamblers, Trustpilot, and forums.
**Pain Point:** Failed payouts drive chargebacks, regulatory scrutiny, and player churn. PayPal "technical difficulties" suggest PSP-side routing failures on payouts.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Smart Routing optimizes payout channels, and transaction recovery automatically retries failed payouts through alternative routes.
**Best Case:** Rappi achieved 80% less analyst resolution time with real-time monitoring, detecting issues in milliseconds vs. 5–10 minutes manually.
**Outreach Angle:** "Your withdrawal complaints on AskGamblers and Trustpilot suggest payout routing issues. Yuno's real-time monitoring detects failed payouts in milliseconds and auto-reroutes — reducing player friction and regulatory risk."

### Insight #3: LatAm Expansion Requires Local Payment Methods

**Evidence:** Section 4B — Brazil, Mexico, Chile, Ecuador markets unverified for local APMs (PIX, Boleto, SPEI, OXXO, Webpay). Section 6 — active LatAm content partnerships and Mexico expansion.
**Pain Point:** Cross-border card processing in LatAm has lower approval rates and higher costs than local acquiring. PIX in Brazil is essential — absence would severely limit market penetration.
**Yuno Value Prop:** New market live in weeks with no-code PSP enablement. Single API → 300+ payment methods across LATAM including PIX, Boleto, SPEI, OXXO.
**Best Case:** InDrive went live in 10 LATAM markets in under 8 months with Yuno, achieving 90% approval rates.
**Outreach Angle:** "As you scale in Brazil, Mexico, Chile and Ecuador, local methods like PIX and SPEI are table stakes. Yuno connects you to 300+ LatAm payment methods through a single API — InDrive launched 10 markets in under 8 months."

### Insight #4: Post-Acquisition Independence = Strategic Reset Moment

**Evidence:** Section 6 — Allwyn acquisition collapsed March 2026. Novibet is now independent with 9+ markets, 1,000+ employees, and growing.
**Pain Point:** The failed deal may redirect focus toward building independent infrastructure capabilities rather than relying on a parent company's stack.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno as a strategic infrastructure partner provides enterprise-grade payment orchestration without requiring acquisition or massive internal build.
**Best Case:** Livelo achieved +5% approval uplift and 50% transaction recovery.
**Outreach Angle:** "Now that the Allwyn deal is off the table, Novibet is charting its own course. Yuno can be the payments infrastructure backbone that scales with you across every market — without the overhead of building it in-house."

### Insight #5: In-House Payments Team = Readiness Signal

**Evidence:** Section 3B — active hiring for Payments Associate and Payments Team Leader in Athens. 67–73 open roles company-wide.
**Pain Point:** Building an in-house payments team signals payment complexity is a priority. But internal teams still need tools — orchestration, routing, monitoring.
**Yuno Value Prop:** Yuno amplifies payments teams rather than replacing them. Real-time dashboards, no-code PSP management, and automated routing free the team to focus on strategy.
**Best Case:** Rappi's payments team went from manual 5–10 minute detection to millisecond automated monitoring.
**Outreach Angle:** "I saw you're building out your payments team in Athens. Yuno gives payments teams superpowers — real-time dashboards, no-code PSP switching, and automated routing so your team focuses on strategy, not firefighting."
