# SDR Research Brief — Hertz
*Yuno Payment Orchestrator | Research date: 2026-06-04 | Framework v8.0 (4 agents, accuracy-first)*

Company: **Hertz Global Holdings, Inc.** (NASDAQ: HTZ). Brands: Hertz, Dollar, Thrifty, Firefly. Vertical: car rental / mobility.

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Hertz runs its global checkout on a **single, confirmed PSP, Stripe**, consolidated in March 2024 across Hertz, Dollar and Thrifty corporate locations in the US, Canada, 11 European countries, Australia and New Zealand (online, mobile app and in-person POS). Stripe is already selling Hertz auth-rate tools (Adaptive Acceptance + Card Account Updater), which proves Hertz cares about approval optimization but is locked to one provider's routing logic with no acquirer redundancy or smart failover. Layer on a high volume of authorization-hold, double-charge and refund complaints (BBB billing issues in the hundreds, examples through Jan to Apr 2026), a 2024 to 2025 breach that exposed customer payment-card data, and an activist-investor turnaround mandate (Ackman 19.8%), and the Yuno opportunity is clear: a multi-PSP orchestration + smart-routing layer for approval uplift, recovery and redundancy across Hertz's multi-region, multi-currency footprint, with local acquiring/APM expansion in International RAC (Europe, APAC, LatAm).

## SECTION 10 — Strategic Insights

**Insight #1: Single-PSP dependency on Stripe with no orchestration layer**
Evidence: S3A/S3B, Stripe is the sole confirmed PSP since 2024; no orchestrator found. Pain Point: one acquirer = single point of failure, no smart failover, no acquirer competition on cost/approval. Yuno Value Prop: multi-PSP smart routing (+7% approval uplift) and automatic failover without re-integration. Best Case: InDrive (10 LATAM markets in under 8 months, 90% approval). Outreach Angle: Hertz already optimizes auth with Stripe's Adaptive Acceptance, but that optimization stops at one provider's edge; a routing layer above Stripe adds redundancy and a second acquirer to compete on every transaction.

**Insight #2: Hertz already cares about auth-rate (Adaptive Acceptance) but is locked in**
Evidence: S7 #2, Stripe is selling Card Account Updater + Adaptive Acceptance to Hertz. Pain Point: approval logic is captive to one PSP, no benchmarking across acquirers. Yuno Value Prop: retry/cascade across providers recovers ~50% of failed transactions; routes by BIN/issuer/geo. Best Case: Livelo (+5% approval, 50% recovery). Outreach Angle: you are clearly chasing approval uplift; the next increment comes from routing across providers, not tuning inside one.

**Insight #3: Authorization-hold, double-charge and refund complaints at scale**
Evidence: S5, hundreds of BBB billing complaints, refund delays (debit up to 30 days), holds stuck. Pain Point: the multi-step rental money flow leaks into duplicate charges and slow refunds, a CX and chargeback risk. Yuno Value Prop: real-time monitors (millisecond detection) + cleaner auth/capture lifecycle reduce duplicate charges and speed resolution. Best Case: Rappi (80% less analyst resolution time, millisecond detection). Outreach Angle: tie the public auth-hold/refund noise to the cost of running one rail with manual ops.

**Insight #4: Activist-led turnaround = mandate to improve unit economics**
Evidence: S6, Gil West CEO + Ackman 19.8% + cost/RPD focus. Pain Point: every basis point of approval and every recovered transaction flows to the bottom line under investor pressure. Yuno Value Prop: approval uplift + recovery are direct revenue, no fleet capex. Outreach Angle: frame Yuno as revenue recovery that does not require touching the fleet or pricing.

**Insight #5: Multi-region International RAC (Europe, APAC, LatAm) with local entities**
Evidence: S2, owned entities across Europe, Brazil, APAC, AU/NZ. Pain Point: a US-centric Stripe setup may under-serve local acquiring/APMs in International RAC where local rails are cheaper and lift approval. Yuno Value Prop: local acquiring + APM enablement (no-code), new market live in weeks. Best Case: InDrive / Reserva (+4% approval in under 3 months). Outreach Angle: International RAC is where local methods and local acquiring move approval the most, exactly where one global PSP is weakest.
